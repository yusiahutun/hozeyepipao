Bruna Biancardi, Neymar’s former girlfriend and the mother of his newborn child, recently announced her separation from the football superstar.

Biancardi addressed the situation through her Instagram Stories at the end of November 2023. Per People, she wrote in Portuguese, “This is a private matter, but as I deal with news, assumptions and jokes every day, I inform you that I am not in a relationship.”

“We are Mavie’s parents, and that is the reason for our bond,” the model added, referring to her new daughter, as per NY Post.

The news emerged just a month after the former couple joyfully welcomed their baby girl.

The same month, Neymar was deregistered from Saudi Pro League club Al Hilal while recovering from an injury.

In November, TMZ reported accusations against Neymar of sending X-rated messages to a Brazilian OnlyFans model. But the footballer denied these allegations, claiming that the leaked DMs were from “years ago”.

Neymar has faced previous accusations of infidelity after being seen on camera in a nightclub earlier in 2023, getting close to two women, as mentioned in the same source.

Here’s everything we know about the forward’s ex-girlfriend.

What is Bruna Biancardi known for?

Beyond her now former relationship with Neymar, Biancardi, 29, is a Brazilian model who has also made a name for herself as a social media influencer.

She has starred in campaigns for luxury brands like Balmain, Louis Vuitton and Off-White. She also has over nine million followers on Instagram.

She jet sets across the globe

Biancardi leads a globetrotting lifestyle. Her Instagram reveals that she’s rarely in one place for an extended period, showcasing her presence in stunning destinations spanning from Paris and the Maldives to Dubai, Qatar and Italy.

She hangs out with Lionel Messi’s wife

Having been a former WAG, Biancardi is used to socialising with other footballers’ partners. According to People, Biancardi appears to have a close relationship with Lionel Messi’s wife, Antonela Roccuzzo. They have posted pictures together in the past few months.

She was targeted in a kidnapping attempt

In November, Biancardi found herself at the centre of a frightening kidnapping attempt, as reported by multiple sources. According to India’s The Economic Times, armed intruders entered Biancardi’s residence in October 2023 and held her parents hostage in Cotia, a city within São Paulo’s metropolitan area.

The report also mentioned that the assailants inquired about Biancardi and her baby girl’s location, but the mother and daughter no longer lived there at the time. A man has since been arrested in connection with the home invasion, states British media.

How long did Neymar and Bruna Biancardi date for?

British media reports suggest that Neymar and Bruna Biancardi have been in a relationship since 2021, although they were more low profile during their earlier months. They reportedly went public with their relationship on Instagram in January 2022.

This isn’t the first time Neymar has been accused of cheating, either. The couple briefly separated in the summer of 2022 before reconciling, with Biancardi dismissing rumours about Neymar’s alleged infidelity during that time.

Biancardi posted: “Don’t believe everything that happens out there. I am very fond of him [Neymar] and his whole family. Please stop involving my name. Thank you.”

How many kids does Neymar have?

Marca reports that Neymar, 31, has two children. He has a 12-year-old son named Davi Lucca da Silva Santos from a previous relationship with Carolina Dantas. And, of course, Neymar and Biancardi welcomed their first daughter together named Mavie in October 2023.
