Is Reneé Rapp Still Dating Antonio Cipriano?

The answer to this is a resounding yes. Reneé Rapp was still dating fellow Broadway star Antonio Cipriano as of the time of this writing.

Meanwhile, the two could be spotted together on social media until as far as 18 August 2021.

They started dating at least since 2019 and have been Instagramming their moments together from that year on. However, unlike other regular couples, they have never attempted any loved-up exchange as such. Instead, they tried to be witty and funny every time they posted anything of them together in a frame. Nevertheless, they certainly gave away the couple’s vibes and appeared to be in love.

Antonio, if you do not already know, is an actor, singer, and musician. He is on Instagram @antoniocipriano_ where he had 145 posts and 64.3k followers, so far. He appears in an episode of beau Reneé’s The Sex Lives Of College Girls. Meanwhile, IMDB also credited him as an actor for 2019’s City on a Hill, and Safehaven, an upcoming American supernatural thriller drama television series.

Antonio originated the role of Phoenix in the stage adaptation of Jagged Little Pill and has made appearances in television series such as God Friended Me and City on a Hill.

In September 2021, he caused a bit of a stir when he quit his role in Jagged Little Pill, after the musical’s non-binary character, Jo, was changed to cis-gender. “I have to acknowledge the harm that many trans + non-binary, and all marginalized folks, on-stage cast members and off have endured through the years … Broadway should be a safe space for everyone to create and experience art. All artists deserve to exist fully and authentically”, he had explained.

Antonio turned 21 on 13 May 2000.
