Wowcher mystery holidays for 99 are back including Dubai, New York and the Maldives

May 08, 2024
blog

Wowcher’s incredible £99 mystery holidays are back – but this time you could be sent away to incredible destinations like the Maldives, Thailand, Dubai, New York, Barbados, Disneyland Paris and Las Vegas.

Depending on when you choose to fly you could bag a trip from just £99 per person including your flights and hotel to one of 100 destinations throughout 2024. The options available – which are assigned to holidaymakers at random – include long-haul and short-haul hotspots.

Holidaymakers who do choose to spend the money on a mystery holiday could end up jetting off on a number of city breaks including those to Venice, Rome, Amsterdam, Disneyland Paris, Madrid, Porto, Lisbon, Krakow, Budapest, Prague, Lake Como and more.

Alternatively, beachside destination are also in the running for those who love to sunbathe and swim from Crete, Ibiza, Portugal, Cyprus, Malaga, Malta and a number of other stunning spots. Some of the most exciting options on offer include the long-haul destinations.

A few lucky shoppers will land the jackpot and end up spending a whopping seven nights in either the stunning islands of the Maldives, over in tropical Thailand or even in beach heaven Bali. There are also seven night stays on offer to Barbados, in the Caribbean, the Dominican Republic, home of great food Mexico and even theme park heaven Florida.

Three night stays are available in other long haul destinations too – mostly big cities that have a big impact. You could walk away with a trip to Dubai, New York, Las Vegas or Toronto, in Canada. The holidays cost £99 per person if you’re happy to fly in February, March, May, or September to December.

While holidays in April, June and July cost £129pp, however keep in mind that the trips are based on two people sharing a room so you will need to go with another person and buy two mystery holidays together.

Mystery Holiday destinations are drawn at random based on the customers preferred travel dates, as provided by the customer within the redemption process. You should be contacted by Wowcher within five working days from the date you complete the redemption process. If you prefer a different date or airport you might have to pay more money (non-London airports cost £20pp more).

It's worth noting that you are not entitled to a refund if you simply don’t like the destination you’ve been assigned. If advice from the Foreign Office changes making your holiday no longer safe then you will be given a new destination. You can find out more and read the full T&CS on the Wowcher website.
