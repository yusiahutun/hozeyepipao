Your horoscope for the week of August 27 to September 2 starts off on a rough note, but leads you toward the most powerful magic.

It all begins when the sun in Virgo forms a harsh opposition with Saturn in Pisces on August 27, revealing everything standing in your way. You may feel stagnant or inhibited by something that’s pushing against you, but knowing your limits will lead you to victory. By understanding what you can’t do, you’re opening your eyes to all the things you can. If there’s a will, there’s a way.

Expect surprises to rain on your parade as Uranus—planet of sudden surprises—stations retrograde on August 28. Although Uranus retrograde is nowhere near as detrimental as the current Mercury and Venus retrograde, it could cause you to unexpectedly change course. Lean into your own impulses and embrace your desire to shake things up in a meaningful way.

Emotions will reach their peak on August 30, when a full Blue Moon in Pisces will bring all your biggest feelings to the surface. This full moon shines a light on the side of our personalities and lives that cannot be quantified or easily explained. After all, Pisces is the sign that sits opposite of rational and logical Virgo, bringing our attention to our most artistic, intuitive, and spiritual selves. Let it all out!
