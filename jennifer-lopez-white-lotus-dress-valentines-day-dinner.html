Jennifer Lopez's latest date night look is very familiar to The White Lotus fans.

For the occasion, the "Waiting For Tonight" singer chose a two-toned Dolce & Gabbana wrap dress that was also worn by her Shotgun Wedding co-star Jennifer Coolidge on the hit HBO drama, when her character Tanya McQuoid attended Quentin's house party at his opulent Palermo villa.

The chiffon knee-length piece features a V-shaped neckline, flowing sleeves, a cinched waist, and light pink and deep salmon pleats. While Coolidge's character wore the dress with minimal accessories, Lopez added a white DG logo belt, finishing the look with gold leather double platform heels, diamond stud earrings, and a square gold-rimmed clutch printed with a geometric heart decal.
