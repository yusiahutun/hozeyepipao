Benny Hinn's profile and net worth

The Israeli-born preacher and author is estimated to be worth $60 million (KSh 8.7 billion, based on the current exchange rate).

Hinn is well known for his faith-healing mega crusades across cities and nations, which are highlighted as his main source of income.

He was born on December 3, 1952, and grew up as a born-again Christian after being introduced to prayer meetings and teaching of the Gospel, Celebrity Net Worth wrote.

He started his healing ministry in Toronto, US by hosting his own evangelical program on local television.

In 1983, Hinn founded the Orlando Christian Center and has authored several best-selling inspirational Christian books.

Where does Benny Hinn's income come from?

Benny Hinn’s impressive net worth can be attributed to his career in televangelism, including healing services, television programs, and books.
