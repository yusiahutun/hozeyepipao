6. You are from different cultures

Why do guys stare at me but never smile? Some habits may be considered normal in some cultures and inappropriate in others. Smiling at strangers, for instance, is not a universal trait.

The guy who is staring at you is probably from a different culture from you. He may want to engage you, but given where he is from, he will not smile. He will only stare and observe from a distance.

Russia, South Korea, India, and Japan are some places where smiling at strangers is not a common practice. Try to understand that this guy is probably not from where you are from, and therefore, his staring at you is just him being respectful and abiding by his cultural values.

7. He is in a bad mood

Everyone knows what it's like to have a bad day. You do not want to talk to anyone, and everything feels dull. This guy staring at you is in a terrible mood and probably has had a bad day. Nothing he looks at is appealing, and everyone sickens him at the moment.
