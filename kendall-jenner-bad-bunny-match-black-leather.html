Kendall Jenner and Bad Bunny Match in All-Black Leather Looks for a Romantic Dinner Date

June 21, 2024
blog

Kendall Jenner and Bad Bunny are taking their date-night style to the next level.

The couple recently enjoyed a night out in Santa Monica, California, where they went on a dinner date in matching all-black leather looks.

The model’s outfit consisted of a black leather tube top and a pair of black flared stretchy mesh pants. She stayed warm in a long black leather trench coat and finished off the look with pointed-toe kitten-heel mules with a grommet buckle and a black-and-yellow croc-embossed Fendi purse.

BACKGRID

Meanwhile, the “Tití Me Preguntó” rapper looked suave in a black leather button-up collared jacket, black leather trousers, and chunky square-toe boots. He accessorized minimally, opting for a black baseball cap worn backwards and a pair of shades with red tinted lenses and a thick black rim.

BACKGRID

Earlier in the weekend, the two made their first public appearance together while sitting courtside at the NBA playoff matchup between the Lakers and the Warriors at Crypto.com Arena.

For the occasion, 818 founder Jenner looked stylish in a white tank top, a furry brown miniskirt, and knee-high snakeskin stiletto boots by Gianmarco Lorenzi. She accessorized with an array of gold accessories, including thick hoop earrings, a square pendant necklace, and several rings.

As for the Grammy winner, he showed up in a black leather jacket, a white button-down, black pants, and pointed-toe boots in a light beige shade. He additionally wore black rectangular shades, a black cap, layers of silver chains, and a bolo tie with a silver bull pendant.
