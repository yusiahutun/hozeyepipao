There's perhaps no moment in life that's as surreal as the one you get engaged to the person you love and deeply desire to spend the rest of your life committed to. It might even feel like a scene out of a country love song.

Once you've had time to savor those initial moments with your new fiancé (isn't it fun to start using that term?), the time will come to share your news. But how do you go about getting the word out to everyone you want to know. When it comes to sharing on social media, we're here to make your job easy with more than 100 clever engagement captions to share your announcement on Instagram complete with a candid photo with your new bling from those first moments or a more styled pic you took a bit later.

Scroll down to peruse these unique sets of words to find the one that feels right to you and the man or woman you are marrying, whether you want a simple one, a witty or funny one, or a non cheesy one. Before you start working on your wedding registry and planning out all the details for your big day (down to the cake topper!), give yourself time to relish in simply being engaged and fully sharing in the joy others have for you too. And now for those caption ideas...
