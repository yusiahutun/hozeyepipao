Heidi Klum isn’t taking the Christmas season off despite vacationing in St Barts when it comes to her modelling stunning looks.

The supermodel set pulses racing with a gorgeous glittering pink gown featuring a deep plunging neckline and thigh-high split. The Project Runway alum is soaking up the sun during the Christmas period but isn’t letting the change of atmosphere take away her festive spirit. But the star didn’t let the festivities fall down by the wayside when it came to her fashion choices for the season.

Heidi Klum enjoys herself in St Barts

The TV host, who turned 50 this year – which no one would be able to tell – posted a series of snaps on social media for the Christmas celebration.

Taking to Instagram, Heidi Klum captioned the post: “Christmas 2023 St Barts,” with a love heart emoji.

She shared with followers her celebrations, long red nails – perfect for the season – and enjoying a dinner with husband Tom Kaulitz. The celebrity took a few sultry snaps looking at the camera, as well as a sneak peek at the cocktail menu she was eyeing up.

Then, Heidi was appearing loved up with hubby Tom as they enjoyed drinks and the celebrity couple snuggled in their booth.

In the final clip, she shimmies down the hall with her sparkling dress and red high heels. But to bring in the Holidays, she also wore flashing lights, draped over her neck, which fell down off the shoulder, resembling Christmas tree lights.

Making the most of the beach

After the festive celebrations, Heidi Klum then shared a cheeky clip of her sunbathing on the beach – without her bikini top.

The blue sky and sparkling sea proved to be the perfect backdrop for the American and German native.

While covering herself up, Heidi Klum showed off the beautiful landscape while laying down on a towel on the St Barts sand. This was before she zoomed in and popped on some sunglasses to protect her eyes from the sun.

Her skin appeared sun kissed, and we can make an educated guess Tom was sitting next to her enjoying the sunshine in adorable Mickey Mouse shorts.

But the couple also enjoyed a hike later on. As the Victoria’s Secret Angel stopped to take a video of their view while climbing up the side of a cliff face, presumably to or from the beach.
