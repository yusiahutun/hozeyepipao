Il Divo Announces A Holiday Song Celebration Tour

July 11, 2024
blog

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – Il Divo has today (July 29) announced their newest tour, A Holiday Song Celebration, will visit intimate North American venues this fall.

Kicking off November 17 in Boston, the tour will stop in cities across the U.S. and Canada including New York, Toronto, Atlanta, Miami, Los Angeles, and more before wrapping December 22 in San Diego.

The quartet will perform songs from their Platinum-certified compilation of holiday classics, The Christmas Collection.

Tickets go on sale to the general public beginning Friday, August 2 at 10 am local time at LiveNation.com.
