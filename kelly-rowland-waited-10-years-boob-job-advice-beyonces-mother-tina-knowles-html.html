Kelly Rowland has revealed she took breast augmentation advice from Beyoncé’s mother.

The singer listened to Tina Knowles, the parent of her Destiny’s Child bandmate, and her own mother Doris before going under the knife.

She added: ‘I took their advice and waited 10 years.’

And even after the X Factor USA judge made the life-changing decision, she still took plenty of time to prepare herself.
