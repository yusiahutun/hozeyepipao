Christie Brinkley and Her Daughters Look Stunning on SI Swimsuit Cover

June 14, 2024
blog

It’s hard to believe that model Christie Brinkley is 63! The mother of three just graced the cover of the annual Sports Illustrated swimsuit issue with her two daughters, and the trio look like they could be sisters.

Brinkley and her daughters Alexa Ray, 31, and Sailor Brinkley, 18, are all color coordinated, and they look downright gorgeous in their black swimsuits.

“My first thought was, ‘At my age? No way!’” Christie explained to People magazine. “When I turned 30, I was like, ‘This is the last time I’m posing in a bathing suit!’ When this issue comes out, I’ll be 63. I thought, ‘Those days are over.’ But to get to do it with my girls, I thought, ‘One last go!’”

The ageless Brinkley first appeared in the magazine in 1979, starring on the cover for the next two years as well. Since then she’s gone on to star in numerous fashion spreads and even a few movies! So even though she reportedly retired from bikini shoots in 2004, she hasn’t missed a beat.
