Cindy Crawford's 1992 Pepsi Commercial Gets Remade in Emojis

April 20, 2024
blog

One fateful day in 1992, Cindy Crawford changed everything when she got a little thirsty and made a trip to a Pepsi vending machine wearing a white tank and high-waisted denim cutoffs. To this day, the supermodel's commercial remains one of the soda brand's most iconic ever, so it comes as no surprise that Pepsi decided to release a remake for 2016.

Giphy

Despite the fact that Crawford basically hasn't aged a day since 1992, Pepsi opted to put a twist on the iconic commercial and transform it into emoji form (it is 2016, after all). Don't worry, though, the remake still features the same teenage boys ogling over the model (or rather, the bottle of Pepsi) and Crawford's emoji even has her signature mole:
