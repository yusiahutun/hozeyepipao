She's become a regular to listeners thanks to her show on BBC Radio 1.

And Mollie King braved the hottest day of the year to head to work at her show's studios in London on Saturday, sporting another fashion-forward look.

The presenter, 36, opted to go braless in a nude knitted vest and a matching linen skirt as she arrived at the radio studios.

Mollie, who welcomed her first child with her fiancé Stuart Broad last November, showed off her svelte physique in the tight cream ribbed vest.

She teamed the top with a high-waisted linen skirt knotted at the waist, and matching heels.
