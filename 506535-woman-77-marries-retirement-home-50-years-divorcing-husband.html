Woman, 77, Marries Herself at Retirement Home 50 Years after Divorcing Husband

May 20, 2024
blog
  • The retiree residing in Ohio had vowed to keep off men and instead opted to say her marital vows to herself
  • She came up with the special concept of marrying herself during one of her visits to her church and received the backing of her family
  • The manager of the retirement home where she resides officiated the wedding that was held at the home's community hall

At 77, Dorothy Fideli has held a unique wedding ceremony to celebrate her own love.

Solo wedding

Fideli exchanged vows with herself in a solo wedding ceremony in a heartwarming celebration of love.

According to Daily Mail, the ceremony was held at O'Bannon Terrace Retirement Home in Goshen, Ohio.

In the presence of her loved ones, the joyful grandmother embraced the fulfilment of her deepest wish: to marry herself.

Having tied the knot in 1965, she parted ways with her husband after nine years.

Fideli stumbled upon the extraordinary idea during a visit to a church and wasted no time in approaching Rob Geiger, a property manager at the retirement home, to officiate the one-of-a-kind ceremony.

She said:

"Something just came over me one day in church that you ought to do something special for yourself. I said, you know what? I've done everything else. Why not? I'm going to marry myself."

Fideli's daughter, Donna Pennington, enthusiastically embraced the idea and took charge of preparing for the event.

The family meticulously arranged for a beautiful dress, catering, and decorations to ensure a memorable celebration.

The big wedding

Fideli admitted to experiencing a mix of nervousness and excitement, much like any bride.
