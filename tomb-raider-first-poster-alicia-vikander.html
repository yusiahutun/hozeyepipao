Alicia Vikander Stars in 'Tomb Raider' First Poster

July 28, 2024
blog

The first official poster of the Tomb Raider remake has been released, teasing Alicia Vikander as the film's archeologist-turned-heroine Lara Croft.

The Oscar-winner's portrayal of Croft will be different from Angelina Jolie's in the 2001 original film. The actress revealed to Entertainment Weekly in July that her role in the reboot finds her famous character just as she begins her tomb-raiding adventures.

"She has all the fierce, tough, curious, intelligent traits, but we’ve stripped away all of her experience. She hasn’t gone on an adventure just yet," Vikander said. "This is a beautiful way of showing a very loved character from more angles."

The differences are perhaps most notable in Croft's new wardrobe. Vikander's iteration ditches Jolie's sexy, all-black attire, never-ending braid, and super-short shorts for a dirtied-up tank top and cargo pants and a messy ponytail.

Courtesy Warner Bros. / Paramount Pictures

Left: Vikander as Croft in ’Tomb Raider’ (2018); Right: Jolie as Croft in ’Tomb Raider’ (2001).

Her costume might be different, but Vikander is training hard for her role nonetheless. She was spotted over the summer with next-level abs, which we assume are the result of doing some of her own stunts.

The Tomb Raider remake hits theaters in March 2018.
