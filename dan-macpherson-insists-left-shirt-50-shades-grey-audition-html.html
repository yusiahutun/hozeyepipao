He's back in Australia to promote his new film Infini, but on Friday Daniel MacPherson let his best kept secret slip.

While chatting to NOVA hosts, Ryan 'Fitzy' Fitzgerald and Michael 'Wippa' Wipfli, the 35 year-old threw them off guard when he went through the latest roles he had auditioned for.

'I went up for Christian Grey,' he said, referring to the lead role in Fifty Shades movie.
