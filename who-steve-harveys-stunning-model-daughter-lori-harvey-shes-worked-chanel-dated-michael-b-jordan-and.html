Who is Steve Harveys stunning model daughter Lori Harvey? Shes worked with Chanel, dated Micha

July 05, 2024
blog

US comedian, author and Family Feud host Steve Harvey, however, was careful to raise his daughter Lori out of the showbiz spotlight. It wasn’t until 2021, when it was confirmed that Lori was dating Hollywood heartthrob Michael B. Jordan, that the 25-year-old started making headlines and getting noticed for her work in fashion and skincare

These days, invitations to glam events are the norm for Lori – as demonstrated by her appearances at Paris Fashion Week, New York Fashion Week, Art Basel parties and the Met Gala.

So, just what do we need to know about this rising “It” girl?

She’s the adoptive daughter of Steve Harvey

Lori, who was born in 1997, joined the Harvey family when her mother, Marjorie Elaine, married Steve Harvey in 2007. It’s unclear who her biological father is, but Steve adopted all three of his wife’s children – Lori, Jason and Morgan. He has four kids of his own, Wynton, Karli, Brandi and Broderick Jr., from his previous two marriages.

“My parents did a good job of keeping us out of the spotlight and letting us have a normal childhood so we could be kids and not live life under a microscope. They let us decide if we wanted to be in the industry,” she told Vogue in 2018.

Lori has openly praised the strength of her parents’ relationship, calling them “couple goals” and saying, “This is what I want one day,” per Us Weekly.

She’s a model and style icon

Harvey made her runway modelling debut in 2017, walking for Dolce & Gabbana. She’s since starred in campaigns for Chanel and Calvin Klein. But the 160cm model has also opened up about her insecurities in the industry, telling PopSugar that when she was just starting out, she “put a lot of pressure on [herself] to be a certain size” and that this took a toll on her mentally.

In July, WWD reported that Lori had signed with IMG Models and WME and will build her business across TV, acting, producing and beyond.

She was an equestrian before she was a model

Harvey initially had dreams to compete with the US equestrian team at the Olympics. She told The Real that she used to ride horses competitively and did show jumping, adding, “That’s what I wanted to do for my career.” She said that she had a whole plan but, when she turned 18, she got into an accident and broke her lower back. She wasn’t able to ride competitively again.

She launched a skincare label – that Kim Kardashian was accused of copying

Harvey launched her skincare collection Skn By LH in October 2021, joining a wealth of celebrity-backed brands in the market.But when Skims founder Kim Kardashian rebranded KKW Beauty to Skkn By Kim in June this year, fans were quick to call out the reality TV star, as the brand name was very similar to Harvey’s. It’s not the first time Kardashian has been criticised for her brand names either. Skims was previously called Kimono, but the billionaire reality star was slammed for the tone deaf name, accused by some of cultural appropriation.

She was a judge on Miss Universe 2021

Like father, like daughter! In 2021, Lori dazzled and looked like she was a contestant herself when she joined the judging panel at the annual Miss Universe beauty pageant competition in Israel, which her dad famously hosts.

“It‘s extra special to me because this is the first event that my dad and I have worked on together, so it’s going to be a really great time,” she said, per Yahoo!

In 2015, Steve became a meme when he accidentally read the wrong winner for Miss Universe that year, resulting in an awkward recrowning moment between Miss Columbia and Miss Philippines (the actual winner) that went viral.

She’s dated some high-profile men – including Michael B. Jordan

Although she’s since become a social media sensation on her own terms, Harvey first landed in the spotlight when she made her couple debut on Instagram in early 2021 with Black Panther star and People’s Sexiest Man Alive 2020, Michael B. Jordan.
