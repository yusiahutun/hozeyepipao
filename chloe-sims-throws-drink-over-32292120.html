Chloe Sims throws drink over sister in explosive foul-mouthed row caught on camera

February 01, 2024
blog

The House of Sims' foundations appear to be crumbling.

Chloe Sims was seen throwing a drink in her sister Demi's face during an explosive foul-mouthed row whicih features in the trailer of their new show. The former TOWIE stars swapped Essex for Hollywood in late 2022 in a bid to make it in the US in their own family show.

The reality programme follows the pair and their siblings Frankie and Charlie as they create a new life across the Atlantic, whilst all living under one roof. Following on from the success of the first series last year, the family returns to the box later this month, with its season two trailer dropping on Wednesday night (March 6).

In a dramatic scene preview crew members are forced to step in and separate the sisters as a wild altercation breaks out in a lavish bar. After telling Frankie and Demi she "f***ing put them where they are" in their careers, Chloe's rage threatens to boil over as she hits out at their "attitude".

Chloe, 42, is soon seen launching a cocktail at younger sister Demi as Charlie steps in in a bid to pull them apart. "What the f***, you f***ing c***," stunned Demi is heard screaming in response - before Frankie calls Chloe a "piece of s**t".

Defending her behaviour, Chloe responds: "You were rude to me, you're my sisters I don't think so!" which prompts Demi to hit out to the rest of the family: "You're embarrassing and a bully. I'm done with her."

The drama isn't over there, however - and bearing in mind this is simply just a preview of what is to come - Frankie declares: "I never want to see her again." Chloe, meanwhile, now accompanied by Charlie as she vacates the scene, declares to her brother that she's "done with both of them".

The series begins in its OnlyFans TV home on March 13 and features the Sims' fellow TOWIE cast member Vas J Morgan and Charlie's fiancée, Georgia Shults. In more relaxing surroundings, the season two preview also shows Chloe and Georgia taking part in surfing lessons before celebrating the latter's bachelorette party.

Offering her thoughts on the new series, OnlyFans CEO Keily Blair said in a statement: "House of Sims has proven to be a huge hit with viewers in the UK and across the world, and we're excited to see people's reactions to an even more dramatic second season.

"We're continuing to strengthen our line-up of reality shows and unscripted series, providing more opportunities for our creators to grow their audience and making OFTV the home of incredible new content for our fans."
