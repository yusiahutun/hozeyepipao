Women's world wrestling champion calls out Logan Paul and brother Jake Paul

Women's world wrestling champion Britt Baker has called out Logan and Jake Paul and opened the door to fight against one - or both - of the boxing brothers.

Anyone who has kept in touch with boxing over the last few years will know that Jake and Logan are no strangers to peculiar match-ups in the ring.

Since turning professional two years ago, Jake has fought - and beaten - fellow YouTuber AnEson Gib, former NBA star Nate Robinson, ex-MMA champion Ben Askren and UFC king Tyron Woodley, the latter on two occasions.

Logan has lost to KSI and enjoyed an exhibition bout with boxing great Floyd Mayweather.

Given some of the Paul brothers' bouts so far, it would be of little surprise to see either of them step into the ring with Baker.

Just like her boxing counterparts, Baker has enjoyed a rapid ascent to stardom in the sports world.

Since signing with AEW (All Elite Wrestling) in 2019, becoming the first woman to do so, she has emerged as a major draw for the firm.

She has held the women's championship since beating Hikaru Shida at Double or Nothing in May 2020.

Her dominance has seen her defend the title on six occasions, most recently against Japanese contender Riho earlier this month.

Having conquered all before her in AEW, it would no doubt be interesting for fans to see how the self-proclaimed "role model" would get on against one of the Paul brothers, but we won't be holding our breath.
