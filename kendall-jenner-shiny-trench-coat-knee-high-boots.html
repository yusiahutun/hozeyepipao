Kendall Jenner Pairs a Shiny Trench Coat with Knee-High Boots

March 19, 2024
blog

Kendall Jenner just put an elevated spin on the model-off-duty look.

The supermodel was seen walking in Beverly Hills yesterday looking so chic in a shiny light brown trench coat with gold buttons from FWRD. Jenner, who is the creative director of FWRD, paired the standout piece with a breezy beige maxi skirt with a high leg slit, a simple white tee from Leset, and black leather knee-high boots with small, bent stiletto heels. (A similar style is available at Stuart Weitzman.)

Jenner additionally accessorized with brown sunglasses and red leather gloves. She wore her straight hair down and seemed to go makeup free.

BACKGRID

The 818 tequila founder's solo outing came after she made her couple's debut with Puerto Rican rapper Bad Bunny at a 2023 Oscars after-party. The two were photographed leaving Beyoncé and Jay-Z's annual fete at Chateau Marmont in the same car around 4 a.m.

Earlier in the night, Jenner joined her celebrity friends at Vanity Fair's after-party. There, she was pictured having fun with younger sister Kylie Jenner (with whom she walked the red carpet) and best friend Gigi Hadid. The two models even shared a sweet kiss for the cameras.

For the occasion, Jenner wore a sparkling gold-and-black scalloped gown from Jean Paul Gaultier's spring/summer 2008 haute couture collection. But for Beyoncé's after-party, she changed into a more casual, sexy look; the model was pictured in a black faux-fur jacket and no pants while leaving the bash with Bad Bunny.
