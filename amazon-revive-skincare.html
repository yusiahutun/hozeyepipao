The Skincare Line We Cant Stop Talking About

September 13, 2024
blog

RéVive is a luxury skincare brand to watch. Founded in 1997, the line uses proprietary “Bio-Renewal Technology” that helps skin look its best with daily use, and some major celebrities have been low-key touting it for years. Since patenting the technology, Dr. Gregory Brown, a surgeon who has worked to heal and repair the skin of burn victims, has packaged a variety of products (in gorgeous bottles and tubes, no less) targeting various issues for all skin types.

RéVive Peau Magnifique Youth Recruit Renewal System

RéVive Peau Magnifique Youth Recruit Renewal System

The pièce de résistance, the product for which RéVive has become renowned, is the Peau Magnifique Youth Recruit Renewal System. This four-week treatment, meant to be applied nightly, firms skin, minimizes lines, and provides nearly the results of an injectable.

There are of course lots of other RéVive products for long-term use in a daily skincare routine as well. The Sensitif line, for example, is ideal for reactive skin that needs a gentler touch; the Intesité line of products, which includes anti-aging serum and moisturizers, is great for regenerating skin cells for healthier, younger-looking skin over time. And one-offs like the Rescue Elixir add a bit of supple hydration on the days you need it most.
