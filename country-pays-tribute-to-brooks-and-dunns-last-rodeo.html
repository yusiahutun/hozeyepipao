Country Pays Tribute To Brooks And Dunns Last Rodeo

May 19, 2024
blog

NASHVILLE, TN (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — Musical guests honored Brooks and Dunn in a tribute show which was televised May 23, as the duo continue their farewell “Last Rodeo” tour. Artists showered the 20-year duo with stories, anecdotes, jokes, and performances of various Brooks and Dunn songs.

The concert featured performances by Jason Aldean, Lady Antebellum, Jennifer Hudson, Kenny Chesney, Sugarland, and Carrie Underwood among others.

The Last Rodeo tour launched on April 23 and is running through August 8.
