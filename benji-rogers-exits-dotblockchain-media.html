Benji Rogers Exits DotBlockchain Media

July 14, 2024
blog

(Hypebot) Serial tech entrepreneur Benji Rogers is exiting DotBlockchain Media, a company he co-founded and served as Chief Strategy Officer and board member. Rogers says he will focus on consulting work, two new ventures and his volunteer role helping to turnaround with PledgeMusic, which he also co- founded.

“I leave our amazing creation in the hands of my co-founders, Chris Tse, Allen Bargfrede and Bill Wilson as well as the brilliant team that we built together, that is being led by my friend and amazing CEO Ken Umezaki,” Rogers wrote. “Under their guidance, these incredible people have pulled together the near impossible and although little has been shared at this point publicly, the behind the scenes work and progress is extraordinary.”

In addition to PledgeMusic, Roger’s new focus in on his strategy work at Lark42 and the launch two new ventures NewsHero and PayChannel.
