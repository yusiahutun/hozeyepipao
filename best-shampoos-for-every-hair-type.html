Best shampoo for damaged hair

Nexxus Keraphix Shampoo

Best shampoo for damaged hair

Nexxus Keraphix Shampoo

Credit: Boots

Nexxus excels at creating advanced formulas with a high-street price tag.

This smart new shampoo contains black rice extract, inspired by the famously well-tressed women of China's Huangluo village, who reportedly wash their waist-length hair in fermented rice water.

Poetic backstory aside, studies have found black rice (more so than white) to contain extremely high levels of the proteins that act as the building blocks of healthy hair, meaning regular use should help rebuild heat-damaged strands.

The formula is satisfyingly rich and creamy and, when used with the matching conditioner and mask, delivers incredibly smooth, soft hair.
