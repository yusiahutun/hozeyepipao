New Book In the Spirit of St. Barths

March 28, 2024
blog

Media Platforms Design Team

Pamela Fiori is following her book In the Spirit of Capri with a tome on another enviable destination: St. Barths. The coffee-table book, to be released by Assouline in April, will cover the best area beaches, villas and wedding destinations, as well as interviews with locals and visitors. Considering the island is a favorite getaway for everyone from Tamara Mellon to Donatella Versace, the book should develop quite the following among the fashion set.

Véronique Hyland is ELLE’s Fashion Features Director and the author of the book Dress Code, which was selected as one of The New Yorker's Best Books of the Year. Her writing has previously appeared in The New York Times Magazine, The New Yorker, W, New York magazine, Harper’s Bazaar, and Condé Nast Traveler.
