Gap x LoveShackFancy Is Here &amp; Selling Out Fast

It’s here. Gap’s long-awaited collaboration with LoveShackFancy is in stores and online and selling out fast. The 76-piece collection went viral when it was seen on TikTok during the Los Angeles launch party, but it wasn’t without its controversies. Many of the items seen there, on Ciara IRL and in the campaign, aren’t actually available to shop. That left some pretty disappointed when they saw the final product. But not everyone since most of it sold out as soon as it showed up online.

The line might not be as bold as people thought but it’s still cozy and cute, with typical LoveShackFancy floral patterns and ruffled silhouettes blended with Gap‘s staple pieces such as denim dresses, khaki pants, baseball caps, and oversize tote bags. Have a kid in your life? There are adorable options for children, too, so you can match.

Here are the cutest items left to shop, with most running from XXS to XXL. Let’s cross our fingers for a restock because we have our eyes on the matching sweats.

  • Floral Hoodie

    An oversized hoodie, in this cool denim-looking floral, is always a good idea year-round.

  • Denim Midi Dress

    This soft denim midi dress is comfortable and cute for any occasion and the tiered skirt with lace detail adds interest to an otherwise basic piece.

  • Floral Tote

    Need a new laptop bag? This soft cotton tote is so cute for school or work.

  • Denim Corset

    Pair this stretch denim corset top with white pants for brunch and a black mini for a night out.

  • Ballet Flats

    The resurgence of the ballet flat is here to stay. These pink flats have a soft satin upper and rubber gripper sole so you can pound the pavement in style.

  • Rosette Pin

    Attach this Rosette Pin to any denim jacket to bring it straight into 2023. It also comes in black and white.

  • Denim Mini Skirt

    This pink stretch denim-washed mini skirt would look so cute with a white tee or crew-neck sweatshirt.
