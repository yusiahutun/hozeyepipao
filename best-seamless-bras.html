What Makes A Bra Seamless?

Edward Berthelot//Getty Images

A seamless bra has molded cups without seams so they have a smooth appearance under your clothing. "The problem we focus on is not just the seams, but every place that a line might show through," Conlin says of Parade's 'edgeless' styles. "Where the band meets your skin, the cup details, even the bands."
