What is Whitney Way Thore’s net worth?

Introduction

Whitney Way Thore is an American reality TV star and fat acceptance movement activist who is best known for starring in ”My Big Fat Fabulous Life.”

Biography

She was born on April 14, 1984, in Greensboro, North Carolina, USA.

Her mother, Barbara, passed away in December 2022. She had cerebral amyloid angiopathy.

In 2002, Thore graduated from Page High School.

Whitney earned her BA in Theatre from Appalachian State University in Boone, North Carolina. During her college years, Thore practiced dance.

After college, Whitney taught English in South Korea.

Later, Thore started to work as on-air producer of the radio show ”Jared & Katie” in the Morning on 107.5 KZL.

She also gained 200 pounds.

”I always had a hard time with my body and always felt fat, even when I was 120, 130 pounds — which is what I weighed until my freshman year of college when I gained 100 pounds,” she told Yahoo.

Thore attributes her tremendous weight gain to polycystic ovary syndrome, a complex hormonal condition that affects 1 in 10 women of childbearing age.

”It’s fascinating that, at least in America, 1 in 10 women are diagnosed with PCOS, and it’s such a varied syndrome that women can have vastly different experiences with it,” she revealed.

In February 2014, Whitney released a series of YouTube videos (“A Fat Girl Dancing”) that went viral. During the videos, she promotes positive body image and body acceptance.

”Within three days and millions of views, I had a manager and live appearances booked for The Today Show, Good Morning America, Huff Post Live, Steve Harvey + more,” Whitney recalled in 2021.

My Big Fat Fabulous Life

@Getty Images

On January 13, 2015, My Big Fat Fabulous Life premiered on TLC.

The reality show chronicles her life as a 380-pound woman.

”She’s an incredibly engaging, funny, and articulate individual who bucks stereotypes,” Wendy Douglas, TLC’s VP of production, declared. She also said: ”We found her No Body Shame message compelling and a unique angle to explore stories related to weight and body image.”

Boyfriend

In seasons 2, 3, and 4 of My Big Fat Fabulous Life, Thore dated Lennie Alehat.

They broke up in 2016 following an alleged cheating scandal.

”Lennie wasn’t for me, but he is a good person and has continued to support me even though we broke up 2.5 years ago,” Whitney posted on Facebook in 2018.

From September 2016 to March 2017, Thore was in a relationship with Avi.

Fiancé

In October 2019, Whitney Way Thore got engaged to Chase Severino at the top of the Eiffel Tower in Paris.

Whitney met Chase through Ryan Andreas, her business partner at NoBS Active.

”Chase is one of Ryan’s best friends from college, and I’m so into him,” Thore said.

In May 2020, Thore and Severino broke up after he admitted to her that he had gotten another woman pregnant.

”After experiencing a lot of ups and downs and still living apart, Chase reconnected with a woman with whom he has had a long history,” Thore explained on Instagram. She also wrote: ”Basically, he said that he had slept with somebody, and it’s someone that I know of, like they’ve been involved before… She is pregnant.”

”Thank you for always supporting us,” Severino announced on his Instagram account. Chase added: ”As we now go our separate ways, I ask for privacy for Whitney, myself, and the mother of my child who wishes to remain anonymous.”

In September 2020, Severino welcomed his daughter, Aurora Joyce ‘AJ’ Severino.

”I’m happy to welcome you into the world, Aurora Joyce Severino,” Chase said for People. ”I love you with all my heart.”

In May 2020, Thore called off the engagement.

Fake Relationship?

Some viewers of ”My Big Fat Fabulous Life” think Whitney’s relationship with Chase was scripted by producers of the TLC series.

”They boarded the plane separately, and Whitney sat in first-class while Chase sat in coach, just a few rows away from me,” one Redditor detailed in a post after the couple’s trip to France. ”They did not meet one another when we deplaned and got their baggage separately at baggage claim.”

Another commented: ”There have been elements of this relationship that seemed real – and others that seem incredibly fake.”

Note – Thore lost about 70-lbs since she and Severino ended their engagement. Some believe that she is preparing for weight loss surgery.

Whitney Way Thore – Net Worth

So, how much is Whitney Way Thore worth?

Thore earned most of her wealth from appearing in 137 episodes of the reality show – ”My Big Fat Fabulous Life.” According to reports, Thore makes around $10k per episode.

In 2016, she released the book – ”I Do It with the Lights On: And 10 More Discoveries on the Road to a Blissfully Shame-Free Life.”

Whitney and Ryan Andreas co-founded No BS Active, a diet culture-free workout program that ”focuses on the joy of movement and the mental and physical benefits exercise has.”

Thore also worked at the North Carolina radio station 107.5 KZL.

Therefore, American reality TV star Whitney Way Thore has an estimated net worth of $2 million.
