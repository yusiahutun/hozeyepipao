Hot cocoa and hot deals coming to downtown Davenport

February 24, 2024
blog

You can sip on free hot cocoa and taste many hot deals at Saturday’s Sip ‘N Shop Crawl in downtown Davenport, from 1-5 p.m.

The same day, 6-9 p.m., Common Chord (at 2nd and Main streets) is celebrating its 20th anniversary of being in the historic Redstone building.

“We are excited to partner with 17 businesses to offer a fun activation for people to explore downtown,” Alisha Espey, program director for the Downtown Davenport Partnership, said Friday. “Saturday is going to be a great day for people to support small businesses during the Hot Cocoa Sip N’ Shop. It will be a great day to bop around downtown.”

You can start your adventure at the Family Credit Union check-in booth located at the Last Picture House (325 E. 2nd St.). Here’s what you’ll snag:

  • Your FIRST cup of FREE hot cocoa to kick off the festivities.
  • A handy map guiding you to all the participating businesses.
  • The Family Credit Union is giving away $25 Visa gift cards to four lucky participants.
  • Stamp card: Make a purchase at five of the participating businesses, get stamped, and you’ll be entered into our prize basket giveaway!

Redstone celebration

Later Saturday, from 6 p.m. to 9 p.m., there will be a free party at 129 Main St., as Zeke’s Island Café celebrates its 10th birthday, and Common Chord celebrates its 20th!

Rude Punch will perform in the Redstone Room 7- 9 p.m., and Lojo Russo will play the Community Stage in Zeke’s 6-9 p.m. There will be an all-you-can-eat buffet by Zeke’s for $20; you can pre-purchase HERE, and free light appetizer selections from The Garrison.
