Hot Male 80s Stars: Leading Men Who've Gotten Better With Age

February 15, 2024

They were the swoon-worthy ’80s stars who defined the decade, gracing the cover of magazines while making all our hearts flutter with desire. Sure it was 30 years ago, but you’ll be surprised how little things have changed! From Tom Selleck to Richard Gere to Denzel Washington see all the hot male 80’s stars you had crush on back when they first hit the silver screen — and probably still do today.

Gere, in particular, says he embraces being a “silver fox.”

“I have never dyed my hair in my life – and I am not going to start now,” Gere said in an interview with Express. “I have had to darken it for a couple of films, such as Unfaithful. It perhaps knocks off five years, no more. But the moment filming is over, I just let it go grey again.”

The 74-year-old Pretty Woman star also doesn’t understand why other celebs are obsessed with reversing the signs of aging with plastic surgery. “It is the same with facial surgery. Why bother with all that stuff?” Gere continued. “I am not kept awake worrying about such things at five o’clock in the morning. I sometimes look around a room and think: ‘Okay, who is not afraid to go grey here? Who is happy to live with the way that they look?’”
