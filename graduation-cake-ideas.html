    It's just about that time of year when young (and not so young) folk across the country celebrate a major milestone by tossing their mortarboards in the air. If you're friends or family with a soon-to-be graduate, you want to mark the occasion with something special. That includes just the right saying in their graduation card, a graduation gift that says "welcome to the next stage of your life," and a real barn burner of a party!

    For that last one you might want to bake them a cake they'll remember. It's far better than just picking up a sheet cake from the store. To help you choose, we've put together 30 of our favorite cakes that are perfect for graduation. They've all got just the right level of fancy and not-too-difficult so you can impress your graduate with a day they'll remember.
