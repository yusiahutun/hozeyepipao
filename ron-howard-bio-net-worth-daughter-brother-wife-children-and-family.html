Ron Howard Biography

Ronald William Howard was born on March 1 1954 in Duncan California into a Hollywood family. What you may not know about him is that he is of German English Irish Scottish and Dutch origin.

In his youth, he made a name for himself by playing the young Opie Taylor the son of Sheriff Andy Taylor in the hit sitcom The Andy Griffith Show. At the beginning of his career as an aspiring actor, Ron Howard acknowledged that he knew he would eventually become a director. One could consider his experience as an actor as a training wheel to better manage men and women in front of the camera. He made his acting debut in 1959 in the film Le Voyage. Then appeared in a few television shows before landing his role in The Andy Griffith Show.

Ron Howard made several appearances on film and television before making his directorial debut in 1977 in Grand Theft Auto a low-budget comedy and action film. The film was only made after Ron agreed to play in Eat My Dust! with Christopher Norris in exchange for a director’s role. Ron Howard then perfected his art by directing several TV movies. In 1982 his big break in directing came when he took over Night Shift with Michael Keaton Shelley Long and Henry Winkler.

Since then he has continued to deliver good and great films including Splash Willow Apollo 13 Rush The Da Vinci Code and A Beautiful Mind for which he won an Oscar for Best Director. Since then Ron Howard has continued to use his talents as a director.

In addition to directing Howard is co-president of Imagine Entertainment a film and television production company alongside Brian Grazer. Imagine Entertainment is credited with some popular projects such as Friday Night Lights 24 and Arrested Development.

Ron’s Parents, Brother

Ron Howard’s mother Jean Speegle Howard was an actress while his father Rance Howard was a director writer and actor. Ron Howard would later follow in his father’s footsteps.

It is said that Ron Howard comes from a Hollywood family but what we did not mention is that both of Howard’s parents’ children followed their parents’ path. Ron’s brother Clinton Howard is also an American actor. He has appeared on many television shows including The Andy Griffith Show which made his brother famous. He also appeared in the cult hit Star Trek. Clinton Howard may not be as popular as his brother but he found his own place in Hollywood history books after participating in many classic projects.

Also a musician Clinton Howard founded The Kempsters in 1981. The group was a new-wave rock and roll group whose name came from the street where the members lived. Clinton married his wife Melanie in 1995; unfortunately in 2017, the couple divorced.

Family: Wife, Daughter, Children

Ron Howard is married to a writer Cheryl Alley. Together the couple has four children – Bryce Jocelyn Paige and Reed. His first child Bryce Dallas Howard is an actress who has performed in major projects such as Spider-Man 3 Terminator Salvation The Twilight Saga: Eclipse. More recently she appeared in Jurassic World and its sequel Jurassic World: Fallen Kingdom in a lead role alongside Chris Pratt. She is married to actor Seth Gabel and the couple has two children together.

Twins Jocelyn Carlyle and Paige Howard were born on February 5, 1985. Paige is also an actress. Ron’s son Reed Cross was born in 1987 and is known to keep a low profile.
