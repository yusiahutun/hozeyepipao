Lionel Messi's name included in Spanish dictionary with the perfect meaning

August 30, 2024
blog

- There is actually an adjective in the Spanish dictionary to describe Lionel Messi

- The word inmessionante is used to describe a player who has flawless ability on the ball

- Messi has been described by experts as arguably the greatest football player

Lionel Messi is so popular with his football prowess in Spain that he now has his own adjective in the Spanish dictionary.

The 33-year-old’s attempt to exit Barcelona and possibly Spain was met with a lot of resistance, and understandably so since he is a brand on his own.

In fact, experts argue the La Liga in general will take a huge hit the day Messi finally decides not to play in the league again.

Throughout his 13 years in La Liga, Messi has broken numerous records which helped cement his place as the league’s greatest ever player.

Among the records Messi has picked in his career include most hat tricks in the Champions League, most goals scored in a calendar year as well as the most goals in La Liga.

Some people use the phrase “league of his own” to describe Messi, and this has been made evident with the fact that the Argentine’s name is actually in the Spanish dictionary.

The word " inmessionante " was included in the Spanish dictionary back in 2013 and it has the most befitting meaning.

The term actually has two definitions, according to the dictionary: “The perfect way to play football, an unlimited ability to self-improve,” and “describes the best player of all time”.

A practical example of the use of the word in a sentence can be “Harry Kane can be inmessionante sometimes when his team really needs him”

Since the coining of the word, it has made Spanish commentators work much easier as they were running out of adjectives to describe Messi’s brilliance over the years.

Back in the side

Ahead of the new La Liga season, Messi seems to have put his differences with the club aside and is willing to see out his contract with Barcelona.

The Argentine has been fully fit and was involved in a number of Barcelona’s pre-season matches.

Barca fans will be encouraged by the fact that Messi linked up quite impressively with Phillipe Coutinho in pre-season.
