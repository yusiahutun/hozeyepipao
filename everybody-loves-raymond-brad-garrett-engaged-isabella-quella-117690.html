Brad Garrett is getting ready to tie the knot — again!

The 56-year-old Everybody Loves Raymond star is engaged to his girlfriend, Isabella Quella, 32, his rep confirmed to [Entertainment Tonight](http://www.etonline.com/news/201719bradgarrettengagedtogirlfriendisabellaquella/)_ on Tuesday, Nov. 1.

Brad and Isabella in September 2016.

In a prior interview, Brad joked about the significant age difference between himself and his love. “I’m with someone now who’s a little young,” he said. “We had an AMBER Alert the other night when we went to the movies.”
