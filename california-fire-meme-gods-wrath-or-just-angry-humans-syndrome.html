California fire meme: Gods wrath or just angry humans syndrome?

July 02, 2024
blog
Sadly, Ohio Republican Party chairman Dave Johnson was probably expressing what many other conservative Christians were thinking when he posted a meme (since deleted) on Facebook, referring to California’s worst wildfire ever (“Volunteer search and rescue teams in California wildfire step up efforts”, November 19) as God’s wrath against a liberal populace: “God’s punishment to liberal California”, and “Hell on Earth brought to you by the liberals in California”.

I don’t believe that God is both angrily retributive as well as compassionately loving, as Judeo-Christianity generally perceives Him, perhaps the same way that an ideal human father might be seen to behave towards his wayward child. However, what if angrily predisposed monotheistic humankind insistently makes God out to be in its own image?
