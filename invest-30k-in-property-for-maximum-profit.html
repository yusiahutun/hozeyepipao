What Are the Risks of Investing in Real Estate?

There are two main risks when it comes to investing in real estate: market risk and property-specific risk. Market risk is the risk that the value of your investment will go down due to changes in the economy or market conditions. Property-specific risk is the risk that your investment will lose value due to problems with the property itself, such as structural issues or a decline in the neighborhood.
