Top 10 highest-paid NHL players in 2021 and their net worth

NHL players receive 13 payments every season. But their salaries have a cap, whereby teams cannot pay their athletes beyond a specific limit. The purpose of a salary cap is to ensure that teams with more revenue do not sign all top players and extend their advantage to those with lower incomes. But even with those limits, the highest-paid NHL players continue to receive millions every year while the lowest-paid players earn less than a million dollars.

For the 2020-2021 season, the NHL team salary cap is $81.5 million. That means no player will make more than $16.3 million in salaries in 2021. In 2020, NHL players agreed to a 20% escrow and accepted a 10% deferred payment. As a result, their income reduced to 72%. Despite this, ice hockey still remains one of the highest paying sports.

So, who are the highest-paid NHL players in 2021? NHL's value keeps improving as it attracts more sponsorship deals. As a result, the players' salaries have been rising throughout the years. Here is a look at the top ten earners, according to Forbes.

1. Auston Matthews - $13 million

Auston Matthews is a professional ice hockey centre and alternative captain for the Toronto Maple Leafs. He was first drafted into the NHL in 2016 and won the Swiss Cup the same year. He became the first player in modern NHL to score four goals in his debut season.

In 2019, the Toronto Maple Leafs signed Matthews to a 5-year contract extension worth $58.17 million. His salary for the 2020-2021 season is $13 million. That makes him the highest-paid NHL hockey player.

2. Mitch Marner - $12.3 million

Mitch Marner is a professional ice hockey right winger and alternate captain for the Toronto Maple Leafs. He made his debut in the NHL in the 2016-2017 season. In 2019, he signed a six-year contract with the team worth $65.358, and the average annual value of the agreement was $10.893 million at the time.

3. Connor McDavid - $10.7 million

Connor McDavid is an ice hockey centre and captain of the Edmonton Oilers. He began his professional career in 2015. Since then, McDavid has won many championships and personal awards. He has become one of the best ice hockey players in the world.

How much money does Connor McDavid make? As one of the NHL highest-paid players, his salary for the 2020-2021 season is $10.7 million. Per Forbes, McDavid makes more money from endorsement deals that will accumulate to $4.5 million per years. That means his total earnings in 2021 is $4.5 million.

Artemi Panarin plays professional ice hockey as a winger and is the alternate captain for the New York Rangers. Panarin started playing hockey in Russia in the Kontinental Hockey League. He signed a two-year, entry-level contract with the Chicago Blackhawks in 2015.

In 2016, he signed a two-year, $12 million contract with the Chicago Blackhawks that took effect for the 2017–18 and 2018–19 seasons. Panarin signed a 7-year deal worth $81.5 million with the New York Rangers. His annual average salary was to be $11.642 million. But for the 2020-2021 season, he will receive $10.7 million.

5. Andrei Vasilevskiy - $9.8 million

Andrei Vasilevskiy is a professional ice hockey goaltender for the Tampa Bay Lightning. The Lightning drafted him in the first round at the 2012 NHL Entry Draft. He has one of the highest contracts in NHL worth $76 million.
