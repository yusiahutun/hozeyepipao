Natalie Portman's timeless Cannes Film Festival wardrobe

September 24, 2024
blog
Marc Piasecki//Getty Images

For her first red-carpet appearance of the 2023 festival – at The Zone Of Interest premiere – the actress opted for some sparkle in the form of this archival Dior dress from the autumn/winter 2017 collection, which featured a beautiful blue ombré effect and twinkling sequins.

Michael Buckner//Getty Images

She added some matching deep blue jewellery from the Chopard Red Carpet collection, in the form of a dazzling necklace and cocktail ring.

Arnold Jerocki//Getty Images

Natalie Portman never sets a fashion foot wrong on the red carpet, but her off-duty style is also worth noting. Pictured walking through the Hotel Martinez, the actress looked effortless in a black trouser suit and oversized sunglasses.

Dominique Charriau//Getty Images

For the May December photocall, the actress opted for some one-tone dressing (one of the easiest ways to look polished and put-together), in a red Dior mini dress and jacket, with matching strappy sandals by Jimmy Choo.

Dominique Charriau//Getty Images

She finished the look with her favourite cat-eye sunglasses – a must for any appearance on the Croisette.

Stephane Cardinale - Corbis//Getty Images

For her second big premiere appearance, Portman walked the iconic red Cannes steps wearing a custom Dior couture gown. The dress, with its delicate scalloped skirt, was a reimagined version of the 'Junon' evening gown, originally created by Christian Dior himself in 1949.

Stephane Cardinale - Corbis//Getty Images

She paired her gown with some classic diamond droplet earrings by Chopard, with her hair in a sleek ballerina bun.

Reynaud Julien/APS-Medias/ABACA/Shutterstock

The actress changed into a black Dior dress with a mesh bodice and sheer, textured skirt, to attend the May December after-party.

Courtesy of Celine

During her time in Cannes, Portman hosted a dinner on behalf of Chopard, matching the lilac floral table setting with her ruched off-the shoulder dress by Celine. She kept warm by draping a slick black tuxedo jacket over her shoulders, also by Celine.

Courtesy of Celine

She once again finished her look with some sparkling Chopard diamonds.
