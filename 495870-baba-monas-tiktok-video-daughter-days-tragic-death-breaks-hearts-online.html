  • Heartbroken Kenyans expressed disbelief as news went around that the TikTok sensation and his daughter was no longer alive

A TikTok video posted by Kevin Oselu, widely known as Baba Mona on social media platforms, just four days before he died in a tragic accident has left internet users heartbroken.

Baba Mona Londiani accident

Baba Mona, his daughter Mona, and two of his sister died on the evening of Friday, February 24, after he lost control of the car he was driving and veered off the road at a spot in Londiani.
