Dakota Fanning in see-through dress with rapper FloRida at NYC event

September 29, 2024
blog

She's made career of playing the girl next door.

But a new side of Dakota Fanning emerged on Monday night.

The 20-year-old donned a sheer crocheted blue dress that left little to the imagination, showing off her black undergarments and slender physique. 

Daring Dakota seemed at ease in her get-up, posing happily with rapper Flo Rida, 35, as they both attended the Accessories Council Excellence - ACE - Awards in New York City.

Flo Rida looked trendy in his patterned black jacket, and ensured to keep his gaze hidden behind a pair of large sunglasses. 

Meanwhile, Dakota's bold blue dress featured a low scoop neck, and though it revealed most of her figure, modestly fell just below her knees.

The I Am Sam actress, who is currently dating model Jamie Strachan, wore her bright blonde tresses in a simple and straight 'do, and opted for minimal make-up, ensuring her piercing blue eyes popped with a coat of mascara.

Though her fingernails remained bared, the actress color-coordinated her blue look with a matching coat of polish upon her toenails. 

Completing the fashion-forward look was a black and white fringe clutch.

The awards paid tribute to a number of people who brought attention to the accessories industry, and Dakota at one point posed beside Proenza Schouler designers Jack McCollough and Lazaro Hernandez.

Dakota certainly wasn't the only head-turning star at the event, as Kerry Washington wowed in her modest, if form-fitting look.

The Scandal actress, 37, looked incredible as usual in a plaid dress that featured polka dot sleeves.

Kerry at one point posed beside supermodel Naomi Campbell, who donned a lovely halter dress.

Nicky Hilton, 31, showed her natural beauty at the awards ceremoney in a multi-coloured floral print mini dress.

Paying tribute: Nicky looked poised as she took a place behind the podium

The ACE Awards were created in 1997 to honor individuals and groups for raising awareness of the accessories industry.

The honorees at the ceremony held at Cipriani 42nd Street included Proenza Schouler for Designer Of The The Year and Scandal star Kerry Washington and costume designer were honoured with the Influencer Of The Year award.

Google Glass also was given an ACE Award for Fashion And Technology.

Past winners have included Kanye West and Lady Gaga as Stylemaker award recipients while Jennifer Lopez, Kim Cattrall, Debra Messing, Joan Rivers and Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen have been ACE Award winners.
