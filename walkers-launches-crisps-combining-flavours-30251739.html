Walkers launches crisps combining flavours of Wotsits, Monster Munch and Doritos

April 05, 2024
blog

When it comes to the humble crisp, a lot of people think you can't beat the classics.

From ready salted to cheese and onion - there are some flavours that truly stand the test of time.

But Walkers may be about to change your mind with its latest foodie creation.

The popular crisp brand recently announced its limited-edition range of "hybrid" crisps that combine the flavours of iconic Wotsits Really Cheesy, Monster Munch Pickled Onion and Doritos Chilli Heatwave products into Classic Walkers Crisps for the very first time.

If the savoury treats sound like they're too good to turn down then you'll be glad to know the first of its kind crisps will be rolled out in supermarkets from June 16.

But you'll need to act fast if you want to sample them as they won't be on shelves for long.

The limited-edition range of crisps will put a big twist on the iconic Wotsits Really Cheesy, Monster Munch Pickled Onion and Doritos Chilli Heatwave products.

For the first time ever, these classic flavours will be available in Walkers potato crisps.

Wotsits Really Cheesy, Monster Munch Pickled Onion and Doritos Chilli Heatwave are a central part of British culture, with millions of loyal fans across the country adoring them.

Now Walkers is bracing itself for some extreme and polarised reactions as people taste the flavour of their favourite snack, but with the texture of a potato crisp.

Times are changing, and so are our most treasured flavour combos!

Jonathan Roberts, Senior Brand Manager at Walkers, said: "Walkers is committed to bringing fun and excitement to the savoury snacks category.

"These limited-edition crisps have never hit the shelves before, and we cannot wait to see people’s reactions as their brain tries to process what they are experiencing.

"But this absurd…ly good twist on Wotsits Really Cheesy, Monster Munch Pickled Onion and Doritos Chilli Heatwave will only be available for a limited time only – grab a pack before they’re gone!"
