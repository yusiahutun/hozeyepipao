People's Choice Awards' best Red Carpet looks - Sydney Sweeney to Billie Eilish

June 17, 2024
blog

Many famous faces stepped out last night at the People’s Choice Awards, and there were so many great fashion looks – we’ve taken it upon ourselves to narrow it down and share some of the ones that had people talking.

During last night’s award show, which gives recognition to what is on both the silver and big screen, many stars were in attendance. From The Hunger Games: Ballad of Songbirds and Snakes star Rachel Ziegler and Sydney Sweeney to Pedro Pascal and Tom Hiddleston, there was a lot of big competition this year. However, it wasn’t only their performances that had people talking, as their fashion choices were a big discussion point online.

People’s Choice Awards – the best Red Carpet fashion looks

Sydney Sweeney

Sydney was in attendance with her Anyone But You co-star, Glen Powell, with whom she has sent the rumor mill about their relationship into overdrive. Sydney was nominated in the Comedy Movie of the Year category.

However, she lost out to Jennifer Lawrence, who won for her performance in the rom-com No Hard Feelings.

She wore a sultry red Mônot dress which was floor length. It featured a slit up the back which allowed for her shows to show as she walked. Her shoes matched her dress and were a set of red high heels. She accessorized with big silver earrings and silver rings on some of her fingers. Syney’s nails were nude, while her makeup was accented by a smoky eye look.

Billie Eilish

Billie Eilish opted to stick with her signature baggy fashion look for the People’s Choice Awards and chose to sport simple colors.

She wore a gray and black sweater west, a cream button-up shirt, and very baggy black trousers. Her shoes were a black pair of FTP sneakers dotted in white.

The music star’s makeup was understated and she wore her eyeglasses while wearing her new hair style down.

Billie was nominated for Pop Artist of the Year but lost to Taylor Swift. She did, however, go home with an award for her TV role in Swarm.

Lenny Kravitz

Lenny Kravitz sported his signature shades during the ceremony’s red carpet.

His look was all-black ensemble. A black blazer with big shoulder pads dressed the top half of Lenny as he skipped wearing a shirt, while leather-look trousers and black boots were his choice of clothing from the waist down.

Lenny won the People’s Choice Music Icon accolade at the 2024 awards.

Cori Leray gives us color on the People’s Choice Awards Red Carpet

Coi Leray gave us a pop of color on the carpet.

The Players rapper wore a lime green dress and paired it with a fluffy jacket of the same shade, while her green heels featured a spiral up her legs.

Her short hair was curled and she sported a white necklace and silver dangling earrings. Her makeup was a classic look with a nude lip and a wash of eyeshadow.

Laverne Cox

Laverne Cox wore a look crafted for her by Mugler. The E! News presenter dinner a black corset top and matching gloves. Her sheer black shirt was covered in crystals and she paired it with black heels.

Laverne’s jewelry was quite the statement too as her white necklace looked like branches wrapping around her neck while her earrings were big silver hoops.

Her makeup look featured a smoky eye to compliment her dark outfit while her honey-colored curls were pulled back in a high pony.

Noah LaLonde

Noah LaLonde made a statement with the color he chose for his suit to attend the awards.

The celebrity wore a satin lilac jacket and pants while his shirt was a simple white. His pointed shoes didn’t take away from the fashion look at the People’s Choice Awards, as they were colored black.
