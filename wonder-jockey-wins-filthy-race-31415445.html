Wonder jockey produces 'filthy ride' to win from impossible spot as fans say 'different class'

February 18, 2024
blog

Horse racing fans were left hailing wonder jockey Billy Loughnane after they watched him ride to an improbably victory at Newcastle on Friday afternoon.

Racing in the 4.45, Loughnane, riding Spangled Mac, looked to have no hope of winning two furlongs out, with the young jockey and his horse at the back of the pack. However, the 17-year-old somehow managed to fly through the field, charging past his competitors to claim an improbably win.

The teenager has enjoyed an incredible season, having recently been named as the 2023 Champion Apprentice Jockey on Champions Day at Ascot last month. And fans on social media were also left stunned by Loughnane and Spangled Mac's 'filthy ride'.
