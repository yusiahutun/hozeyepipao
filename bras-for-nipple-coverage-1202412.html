    Things to Consider Before Buying Nipple Concealing Bras

    Bras for nipple coverage could be of many types such as wired bras, minimizer bras, seamless bras, sports bras, soft-padded bras, etc. And while you will shop for a bra category that suits your needs, there are a few factors to consider when shopping:

    Comfort: This is a non-negotiable feature in wearables, especially undergarments. Uncomfortable bras nullify the bottom line of wearing bras at all and cause severe physical damage in the long run.

    Cup Size: Most complaints related to bras are often rooted in using the wrong size. Wearing the wrong cup size can cause discomfort, poor posture, breast pain, spine pain, stretch marks, and serious health problems such as premature sagging. Hence, always measure your bust size and buy bras that fit that size.

    Pro Tip

    In case you purchase a smaller size, a simple trick is to use boob tapes. It prevents spilling and you can easily use a bra with a smaller cup size.

    Moisture-wicking: Bras, especially sports bras, come in direct contact with perspiration. Hence, moisture-wicking bras help keep your skin cool, dry, and comfortable.

    Breathability: Bras with lightweight breathable fabric and ventilation pores are excellent in enhancing airflow for breezy comfort.

    Anatomical Design: Along with the correct bra size, look for undergarments with an anatomical design that responds to your body’s natural curves and contours for a seamless fit.
