Black Friday fashion deals started earlier than ever this year. And now that the sale holiday is in full swing, I've tallied up several hundred sales across the internet from bag deals to bra deals, plus standalone sales at brands like Reformation, Alo, Dôen, and more. That’s a lot of virtual clearance racks and slashed prices to sort through, all while preparing for the long weekend.

To get the highest quality Black Friday designer deals in your carts faster, I asked Bazaar editors to weigh in on the sales they’re actually shopping over Cyber Weekend. These editors’ picks are the best of the best in terms of labels included and depth of discount. (It’s our job to hunt down the rarest deals, after all.) Some Bazaar fashion editors are leaning all the way into their job titles with runway selects from Net-a-Porter, Shopbop, and SSENSE; others are going the practical route by refreshing their basics at Mango and ThirdLove. A few team members aren't even thinking about their closets—instead, they're looking to refresh their entire home with savings at Great Jones and Lovesac.
