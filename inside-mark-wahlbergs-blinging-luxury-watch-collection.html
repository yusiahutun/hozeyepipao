Inside Mark Wahlbergs blinging luxury watch collection, from the Patek Philippe Nautilus Tiffany

One of the most recognisable and eye-catching of the Daytonas, this timepiece boasts 36 baguette-cut sapphires adorning its bezel, each with a different hue, arranged to create a rainbow-like effect. Complementing the bezel decoration are baguette-cut sapphire hour-markers. Wahlberg is in good company with his 18k yellow gold timepiece, as celebrities including Adam Levine, Kevin Hart and John Mayer have all been seen sporting iterations of the Daytona Rainbow.

Patek Philippe Nautilus 5711/1A-010 Tiffany & Co.

One of the most sought-after timepieces to enter the market, Wahlberg has been seen proudly sporting the Patek Philippe Nautilus Tiffany & Co. With its trademark blue and co-signed dial, this limited-edition watch is one of just 170 made – released in celebration of Patek Philippe and Tiffany & Co.’s 170-year partnership. Commemorating the long-standing relationship, the crystal caseback is engraved the words “Tiffany & Co. – Patek Philippe 170th anniversary 1851-2021”. The watch retailed for over US$52,000 but fetched a staggering US$6.5 million at auction in December 2021.

Rolex Day-Date 228349RBR-0036

The Daytona Rainbow isn’t the only blinged-up piece in Wahlberg’s collection. He also owns an 18k white-gold Rolex Day-Date 40 that uses baguette-cut diamonds and sapphires as hour markers, while pavé diamonds adorn the dial and decorate the bezel. The timepiece is finished with an oyster bracelet.

White gold Patek Philippe Chronograph Ref. 5172G-010

Wahlberg’s Patek Philippe collection includes a recent addition – the white gold Chronograph Ref. 5172G. Introduced to the public during Watches and Wonders 2022, the timepiece features a 41mm, 18k white-gold case. The salmon dial features grey-gold numerals treated with a luminescent coating, and a tachymeter scale. The caseback is made from sapphire crystal, and the case is complemented with an alligator strap finished with a fold-over clasp.

Patek Philippe Sky Moon Tourbillon Haut Artisanat

According to reports, in 2021, Wahlberg bought a Patek Philippe Sky Moon Tourbillon. The 18k rose gold timepiece features a 44mm-wide case and boasts 12 complications, including a perpetual calendar, tourbillon and moon-phase display. The dual-face case boasts delicate cloisonné design work and a celestial chart on the back case and is one of Patek Philippe’s most complicated wrist watches. Each handcrafted timepiece is said to have taken more than 100 hours to complete.

Rolex Oyster Perpetual 124300 Turquoise Dial

It may look like a familiar shade of blue, but this wristwatch isn’t the highly coveted Tiffany & Co. Patek Philippe timepiece. That doesn’t mean it isn’t highly sought-after though – the Rolex Oyster Perpetual with a turquoise dial was released in 2020 and its value has skyrocketed in the years since. Celebrities including Wahlberg, LeBron James and Tunku Ismail Idris, the crown prince of Johor, Malaysia, have been seen sporting the watch.
