Who is Tim Curry?

He is a renowned personality in the entertainment industry. He is a voice actor, composer, TV producer, and singer. Tim was born on 19th April 1946 in Grappenhall, Cheshire, England. He is 77 years old as of 2023.

His mother, Patricia Curry, was a school secretary, while his father, James Curry, was a Royal Navy chaplain. Unfortunately, his father succumbed to pneumonia in 1958, while his mother died in June 1999.

He was 12 when his father died. The actor was raised alongside his elder sister Judith Curry. She was a concert pianist who died from a brain tumour in 2001.

After his father's death, his family relocated to South London, where he attended a boarding school before transferring to Kingswood School in Bath, Somerset. Later, he joined the University of Birmingham for a Bachelor of Arts in English and drama.
