Inside Soft Services 2024 Body Retinol Launch: Software Update

August 18, 2024
blog

The buzz of body retinol is still alive and well, especially now that beauty brand Soft Services may have released the most potent version yet: Software Update Performance Retinol Serum, available now.

Soft Services

A closer look at the Software Update Performance Retinol Serum.

Soft Services

Software Update is best applied during your nightly rotation to areas like the décolleté, arms, stomach, butt, and legs before your go-to body lotion. The formula has a creamy, yet lightweight texture that absorbs quickly, and works just as quickly to encourage cell turnover. As retinol is extremely sensitive to light and air, the serum is housed in a recyclable plastic packaging system with a built-in airless pump that doesn’t even require disassembly.

“When we’re designing products at Soft Services, our goal is for the end product to have it all—formula efficacy, accessible pricing, easy and delightful user experience (product format, texture, aesthetics)—and be as sustainable as possible,” Soft Services founder and CEO Rebecca Zhou tells Bazaar.

Many retinols for the body are formulated with 0.1 percent of the pure ingredient, but Software Update contains 0.25 percent—making it (as you’d guess) around 2.5 times more effective than those products, but still safe to use, according to dermatologists and Soft Services’ clinical tests. After being advised that retinol is effective above 0.25 percent and safe below 1 percent, the brand began the formulation process for the new serum with 0.25 percent as the floor.

“We originally wanted to go higher, but after many cycles of formulation, landed on 0.25 percent as the sweet spot between delivering efficacy and minimizing adverse experiences,” Zhou says. “‘With great power comes great responsibility’ feels fitting to say about retinol.” Software Update’s formula is also blended with 5 percent brightening niacinamide and 3 percent glycerin, to help keep skin hydrated while reducing fine lines and dullness.

As with any new retinol product, experts recommend starting out slowly and incorporating this body serum into your routine once a week, to ensure it doesn’t irritate your skin. Over time, you can increase your regular usage to up to four days a week. To maintain your transformative beauty rest results over the long term, bookmark Software Update with a morning application of at least SPF 30.

Software Update Performance Retinol Body Serum is available for $44 at softservices.com.
