Sydney Sweeney wears Angelina Jolie's 2004 Academy Awards gown to the 2024 Oscars after-party

September 16, 2024
blog

Sydney Sweeney stepped out in a piece of red-carpet history last night, attending the 2024 Oscars after-party wearing a dress previously seen on Angelina Jolie.

Jolie originally wore the gown, which was created by the American designer Marc Bouwer, to the Academy Awards back in 2004. The elegant ivory dress features delicate ruching around the waist, a plunging neckline and an entirely cut-away back.

WWD//Getty Images

Jolie wearing the Marc Bouwer dress to the Oscars in 2004

KMazur//Getty Images

Exactly two decades later, Sweeney breathed new life into the archival style. Working with her stylist Molly Dickinson, the Euphoria star chose to accessorise in a similar way to Jolie, with the same matching white sash and layered necklaces by Messika (a custom design created specially for Sweeney). Dickinson took to Instagram to thank Marc Bouwer and the brand's president Paul Margolin for the dress, writing: "Thank you so much Marc + @paulmargolin for letting us into your archives for this special moment!"

Karwai Tang//Getty Images

Sweeney wearing the same style in 2024

Amy Sussman//Getty Images

Sweeney wasn't the only star to celebrate vintage fashion on Oscars night. Jennifer Lawrence arrived at the after-party wearing an archival Givenchy dress from the autumn/winter 1996 collection by John Galliano; Margot Robbie changed into a '90s Mugler corset; and Carey Mulligan walked the red carpet wearing a recreation of a Balenciaga gown from the 1950s.
