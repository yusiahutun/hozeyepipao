Odell Beckham Jr Quick Biography

On November 5, 1992, the NFL Wide Receiver was born Odell Cornelious Beckham Jr. in Baton Rouge, Louisiana. During his childhood, he had his eyes on various sports, including basketball and athletics.

For his education, Odell attended the Isidore Newman School in New Orleans before transferring to Louisiana State University, where he played college football. From his father, who also played in his early years, he learned everything you need to know about the game.

During his college years, Odell Beckham Jr. was named a freshman of the All-SEC selection. He continued to make great runs in football until he made it to the NFL Draft 2014, where he was selected by the New York Giants.

During his career to date, he has been awarded the NFL Offensive Rookie of the Year when he started in 2014, as well as the PFWA All-Rookie Team. He made it to 3 pro bowls and a 2-second team All-Pro.

Parents, Brother, Cousin

Odell Beckham Jr. is basically a product of his parents. Not in a biological sense, but in an athletic sense, because he has inherited his athletic abilities from his family.

His father, Odell Sr., once played as a running back when he was at LSU. That was in the 90s, and after he finished his education, he played in the Arena League for some time before he dropped out. Just like his father was an athlete, so was his mother, Heather Van Norman, who did athletics during her school days. In fact, she could have made it to the Olympics, but when she trained for the 1992 event, she found out that she was pregnant with Odell.

Heather Van Norman, who was dominant in athletics competitions during her high school days and won 3 NCAA outdoor titles among others, would establish herself as a coach on the track. She has coached several teams, including Green Wave athletes and Nicholls Athletics.

What makes things even more interesting is that his stepfather Derek Mills was also a runner who represented the United States at the Olympics. He won a gold medal at the 1996 event.

Other members of his family are his younger brother Kordell Beckham, born in 2002, and his sister Jasmine Beckham. He has a cousin, Terron Beckham, who also has an eye on the NFL.
