A mom has shared the fierce backlash she faces over calling her daughter Poot, with some haters branding it the 'ugliest name ever,' and others claiming the baby will be 'bullied her whole life' for it.

The mother-of-one often shares videos of herself and adorable little Poot to TikTok, and she has wracked up more than 10 million followers on the platform. 

And although most of Abbie's viewers appear to enjoy watching the mother-daughter duo and usually flood the comment section of her videos with positive responses, some of them have recently started to slam her for her interesting choice of nickname.
