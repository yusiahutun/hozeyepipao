Man City WSL star sent off for tackle that &quot;should be two red cards&quot; against Man Utd

July 21, 2024
blog

Manchester City Women's star Georgia Stanway was sent off in Saturday's derby with Manchester United for a dangerously-high challenge on Leah Galton.

City are currently leading 1-0 in their clash at Leigh Sports Village, with Khadija Shaw's goal seven minutes before the half-time break firing the visitors in front.

The opener from Shaw came just three minutes after Gareth Taylor's side were reduced to 10 men when Stanway was brandished a straight red card.

She received her marching orders after producing a shocking tackle on Galton, missing the ball entirely and sending her studs straight into the United star's thigh.
