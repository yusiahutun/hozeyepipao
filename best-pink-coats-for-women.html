8 best pink coats for women 2023: Longline to puffer coats, teddy fleece coats &amp; more

February 15, 2024
blog

Pink has without a doubt been the colour of 2023 – and pink coats are the latest trend that we're loving. 

From Princess Kate to Gigi Hadid, Amanda Holden and Zendaya, so many celebrities are getting behind the pretty pastel trend – and a pink coat is a perfect way to add a pop of colour to your winter wardrobe. 

Celebrities wearing pink coats

How to wear a pink coat

Pink is just as wearable in the colder months as it is in the summer, and it's the perfect way to inject some colour into an otherwise simple outfit. Whether you want to make a statement with a hot pint coat or keep it subtle with a blush pink, the Barbiecore style can be worn with a midi dress and heels, or styled with leather trousers and boots. 

From H&M to ASOS, Karen Millen and more, we've rounded up the best pink coats that are guaranteed to score compliments this winter and beyond.

How we chose the best pink coats for women

  • Variety: Whether you're looking for a pink teddy coat, a longline coat or a pink puffer jacket, I've chosen a range of different styles in hot pinks, blush pinks and mauve shades. 
  • Price: I've selected a variety of price points ranging from affordable to expensive, so there's something for every budget. 
  • Trusted brands: While I haven't tried on all of the pink coats in the list, all of the brands included are known and loved by myself and the HELLO! team, so they should be of the highest quality. 
  • Availability: Fashion moves fast, but all of the pink coats selected in this edit are in stock, in almost every size.

Best pink coats for women this winter

  • H&M Double-Breasted Pink Coat

    Editor's Note

    H&M's cosy pink coat is trending for a reason. Available in sizes XS-XL, the blush pink style is soft enough to work well with most shades, while still bringing some colour into your outfits. We'd style with with a pair of wide-leg linen trousers, an oversized knit and loafers to complete the look. 

    Made from woven fabric, H&M's blush pink longline coat is cosy for winter without lacking on style. Finished with notch lapels, concealed press studs at the front and discreet side pockets, the staple style is selling out fast, and we don't expect it to be in stock for long.

  • Hobbs Wool Pink Coat

    Editor's Note

    This blush pink coat looks like it could be designer with its slightly oversized fit and gorgeous wool finish. Suitable for day and night wear, the outerwear is great for layering with jeans or trousers during the day, and it will be your go-to evening coat for wearing over dresses.

    ​​​​

    Hobbs is filled within timeless fashion pieces, and the Isla single-breasted coat looks so warm. Made from Italian wool with large front pockets, the coat may be worth the investment if you're looking for a staple pink coat to wear for years to come.

  • River Island Oversized Pink Coat

    Editor's Note

    Pale pink is probably the most versatile when it comes to pink clothing, and River Island's longline piece can be worn with just about any outfit. We recommend opting for black trousers and a blouse for a classy workwear style, or elevate the look with a pair of silver trousers and ballet flats ready for party season.

    River Island's double-breasted coat features an oversized fit, notched lapels and a button fastening. Available in sizes 6-18, the pale pink coat is so wearable for work, daywear or evening wear.

  • Very Bouclé Pink Coat

    Editor's Note

    Bouclé is a favourite style amongst the royals, with Princess Kate and Meghan Markle opting for the timeless style on several occasions. Very's bubblegum pink coat is so pretty with its gold detailing, and the bouclé design definitely gives it that extra something.

    Part of Very's Lucy Mecklenburgh collection, the formal longline coat is so stylish with its bouclé design and glossy gold-tone buttons. Finished with a structured collar and buttons on the cuffs, the bubblegum pink style looks far more expensive than it is. 

  • Hobbs Pink Longline Coat

    Editor's Note

    Hobbs' fuchsia pink longline coat is perfect for adding some colour into your wardrobe while still being an investment piece that you'll wear over and over. We love it styled with leather trousers or work attire, and it'll elevate your winter evening outfits when styled with a dress and heels.

    We're obsessed with the colour of Hobbs' Carine coat. Made from wool, the elegant coat is so timeless with its double-breasted design and side slip pockets, and it's available in sizes 6-18.

  • ASOS Tailored Pink Coat

    Editor's Note

    Y.A.L's hot pink longline coat is so on trend, and it would look so glam styled with a pair of wide-leg trousers, matching pink heels and a sparkly clutch for an elevated evening style.

    ASOS's Barbie pink tailored longline coat is the perfect pop of colour for any winter wardrobe, featuring a relaxed fit, a notch collar and waist tie that cinches the waist. It's available in sizes 6-16, and it's currently 59% off in the sale.

  • Karen Millen Italian Wool Pink Coat

    Editor's Note

    The structured fit on Karen Millen's pink longline coat makes it perfect for work and evening wear, and we're in love with the delicate button detailing on the cuffs. It's available in sizes 6-16, and it's currently on sale.

    Karen Millen never disappoints when it comes to classy fashion pieces, and the pink wool coat is the ultimate evening style. It's woven with Italian virgin wool for a luxurious feel, and cut with a double-breasted silhouette and notched lapels for a tailored fit.

  • Boden Textured Pink Coat

    Editor's Note

    Available in sizes 4-22, Boden's stylish pink coat is one that you'll want to wear every winter. We love it styled with leather trousers and chunky boots, but it would also look amazing with a knitted jumper and mini skirt for an effortless ensemble. 

    Boden's wool blend coat comes in the most amazing magenta shade, and the semi-fitted tailored silhouette looks so expensive. Perfect for winter layering, the pink coat is more versatile than it looks, and we think it would work well with just about any outfit.
