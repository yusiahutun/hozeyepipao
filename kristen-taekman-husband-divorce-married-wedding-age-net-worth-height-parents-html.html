American model Kristen Taekman and her husband kept their wedding vows alive when their marriage was prosecuted in front of the public.

In the article, let's take a look at the current status of their marriage!

Kristen's Family Life, Infamous Incident

Kristen Taekman, who possesses the height of 5'10" (1.78 m), married on October 23, 2003, to Josh Taekman, the co-founder of the supplement company Vitalize Labs LLC, which produces the energy drink EBOOST.

They are parents of two children.  

The Connecticut native and her husband lived a couple of years as a two-person family until Kristen gave birth to her first child, son Cassius Taekman in 2010. 

Their happiness further increased when the couple welcomed their first daughter Kingsley Taekman in 2013.

The family of four was living the perfect family life until Josh's terrible secret was revealed on Tabloids.

The e-magazine, Daily Mail first reported the news of Josh's infidelity in 2015.

Reportedly, Josh was revealed to be a regular user of the extra-marital affair website, Ashley Madison, after the company's website got hacked.

Josh had apparently used a fake username Mr_Big_NYC, and had written in his profile:

"Looking to enjoy the finer things life has to offer. Want to find a woman who likes to be engaged socially and intellectually. No stress and no pressure."

Apparently, he had first signed up when Kristen was pregnant with her second child. It was revealed that had paid for 62 transactions.

Rejection, Wife Defends

Josh initially rejected the claims, but after detailed information was posted online, he eventually succumbed to his guilts and accepted his mistake.

Josh said in his statement to US Weekly,

'I did sign up for the site foolishly and ignorantly with a group of friends and I deeply apologize for any embarrassment or pain I have brought to my wife and family. We both look forward to moving past this and getting on with our lives.'

The Farmington born star was initially terrified of her husband, but after harsh comments started to be thrown at Josh, she came forward to defend her infidel companion.

Talking to The Daily Dish magazine, Kristen said, 

"My relationship is stronger than ever and I think it's good. I think that every relationship for sure has its ups and downs whether it's marriage, dating, friendships. I've had my ups and downs with friendships too. Yeah, I think it's good and it does make you stronger."

Further, she also shut down the haters who wanted the separate the couple and demanded that Kristen should divorce her husband.
