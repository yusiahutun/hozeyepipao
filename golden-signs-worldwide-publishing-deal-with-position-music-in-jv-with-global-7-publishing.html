Golden Signs Worldwide Publishing Deal With Position Music In JV With Global 7 Publishing

May 15, 2024
blog

L-R: T. Bacon (President & CEO, Position Music), M. Molina (CEO, Global 7 Publishing), Z. Perilman (CEO, Global 7 Publishing), D. Powell (VP of A&R, Position Music), M. Chipello (Partner/Head of A&R, Position Music) [Photo: Delia Bush]

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – In a groundbreaking announcement, Position Music President and CEO Tyler Bacon revealed today that Golden, the multi-faceted musician, writer, and Grammy-nominated producer, has secured a worldwide publishing deal with Position Music. This pivotal agreement, forming part of a joint venture with Global 7 Publishing, marks a significant milestone in Golden’s career.

Originating from Barranquilla, Colombia, Golden’s musical odyssey epitomizes self-taught ingenuity and unyielding dedication. Discovered by seasoned music producer Mike Molina and veteran manager and A&R Zev Perilman, Golden’s talent swiftly captivated industry attention, leading to a fruitful collaboration with Global 7 Entertainment.

Golden’s recent triumphs underscore his dynamic versatility and undeniable skill. His co-production on two tracks from the ¿Téo? album Luna in 2023, along with the chart-topping success of “Double Back,” a collaboration with R&B artist Coco Jones, propelled him into the limelight. The latter surged to the Top 20 on US Urban Radio and contributed to the album’s nomination for Best R&B Album at the 66th Annual Grammy Awards.

Teaming up with London On Da Track, Golden’s latest endeavor includes co-producing “Dolla” by Shenseea from her highly anticipated album Never Gets Late Here, scheduled for release on May 24.

“For the past year and a half, Delmar Powell and the rest of the team at Position Music have shown me immense support and provided me with some amazing opportunities,” says Golden. “Position alongside Global 7 has helped me to tap into elements of my creativity in inspiring new ways. I’m hyped to now officially be with them. They’ve been a dream come true — consistently attentive and affirming the countless hours spent making this music. This opportunity gives us all the chance to share my music with a wider audience, to see my songs connect deeper with the world, and to reach new heights in my career.”

“We’re really looking forward to continuing to work with Tyler, Mark, Delmar, and the rest of the Position Music family for this next chapter in Golden’s career. They’ve built a remarkable business and team that I believe perfectly complements Golden’s skillset and career trajectory. Golden is a one-of-a-kind, once-in-a-generation producer, writer, and artist, and I can’t wait to see what Position and Global 7 can do together to elevate his career in this next chapter,” says Molina.

“Position’s multi-faceted A&R team is a great fit for Global 7 Publishing’s debut signing, Golden. Golden is a special talent who thrives across genres and wanted a partner who could accelerate his success across the board. We’ve been impressed by their support from the beginning and understanding of Golden’s vision, drive, and passion. Their culture and approach to publishing are exactly what Global 7 Publishing was looking for in a JV publishing partner, and we’re really excited to build with them. Having someone as talented and ambitious as Golden in our family has been a blessing and we couldn’t be more excited for what lies ahead,” says Perilman.

Golden’s addition to the Position Music roster aligns seamlessly with the company’s ethos of nurturing diverse talent across various genres with a roster that includes Audien, Tinashe, Evalyn, Joe Kearns and more.

Delmar Powell, VP of A&R at Position Music, said, “We’re thrilled to welcome the young and talented Golden to the Position Music family. Golden’s unique sound and innovative approach to music production and songwriting make him a perfect fit for our roster. We’re excited to collaborate with him and support his artistic vision as he continues to create impactful music across all genres.”
