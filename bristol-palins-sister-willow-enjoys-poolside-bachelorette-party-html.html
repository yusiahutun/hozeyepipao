Bristol Palin's sister Willow enjoys a poolside bachelorette party

March 12, 2024
blog

Willow Palin is celebrating the Fourth of July early with a lavish, and very cheeky, bachelorette party weekend thrown by her mother Sarah and her sisters Bristol and Piper. 

The Palin women headed to their home in Scottsdale, Arizona, for the 23-year-old bride-to-be's 'last fling before the ring' bash and bridal shower, which kicked off on Tuesday morning. 

'I'm gonna cry,' Willow wrote on Instagram Stories. 'My mom and sisters went all out for my bachelorette!'

With the help of party planner Tiffany Weaver-Waldrip, Willow's mom Sarah, 54, and her sisters Bristol, 27, and Piper, 17, decorated the home with plenty of personal touches while keeping with the fête's desert theme.

A picture of Willow and her fiancé Ricky Bailey was blown up for the occasion, and there were also numerous framed photos of the couple scattered around the kitchen.

Guests were treated to a mimosa bar, a wall of doughnuts, cupcakes, and gift bags with 'Scottsdale Before the Veil' splashed across the front.     

Willow, meanwhile, was gifted a white one-piece swimsuit with 'Bride' written in gold on the front, a white sash that said 'Bride to Be,' and a straw sunhat that had 'Bride' embroidered in black.
