A former budding footballer insists being a porn star is "not as good as people think" as he opened up on being a "butt-naked sweating mess" on his first ever shoot after moving into the adult film industry.

Damian Oliver previously had high hopes of playing in the Premier League after earning a spot in Crystal Palace's academy. The former talent didn't quite make the grade and actually ended up in prison for a spell before later starting a new career in porn.

He initially got into porn on a part-time basis and would work it round his main job as a painter and decorator. Oliver has since gone full-time after he began dating fellow porn star Sophie Anderson, but admits the job is not all it's made out to be as he lifted the lid on his awkward debut on camera.

"We had to keep cutting, I had her bent over the sofa but I kept slipping all across the floor, they had to keep wiping the floor down, I was just a dripping mess."

"I was good at both of those things, but being a porn star, I thought that would be just as good. But it's not as good as people think."
