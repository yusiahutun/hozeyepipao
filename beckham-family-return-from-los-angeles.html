David and Victoria Beckham return home to London following their summer in LA

September 04, 2024
blog

    The Beckham family's fun-filled summer in Los Angeles has come to an end. David and Victoria Beckham were seen arriving with their brood at LAX airport on Monday as they prepared to fly back home to London.

    Doting dad David led his family into the airport while carrying four-year-old Harper in his arms and holding hands with his son Romeo. The 12-year-old continued to show that he is following in his dad's stylish footsteps wearing black skinny jeans and a striped t-shirt, with his hair pulled back into a small ponytail.

    The Beckhams arrived at LAX airport on Monday

    Victoria walked behind with ten-year-old Cruz, and looked as chic as ever in a navy jumpsuit and black heels for their long-haul flight back to the UK. Meanwhile eldest son Brooklyn followed the rest of his family wearing a t-shirt, jeans and his favourite Kanye West for Adidas Yeezy trainers.

    The trip back to the UK came less than 24 hours after Brooklyn walked the red carpet at the MTV Video Music Awards on Sunday, showing off his style credentials in a Ralph Lauren blazer and skinny jeans. The teen has enjoyed a summer to remember in the States, which has included spending lots of father-son bonding time with his dad and passing all of his GCSEs.

    The family have spent the summer in Los Angeles

    Indeed the whole family appear to have enjoyed their summer in California judging by their social media posts which have included a "magical" day out at Disneyland together as well as family hiking trips and time catching up with friends.

    David has also added to his growing number of tattoos while over in the US, proudly showing off a new "Buster" tattoo in tribute to his first-born Brooklyn on Sunday. The father-of-four also unveiled a new eagle tattoo on his side as well as having the words "Pretty Lady" inked above a pre-existing Harper tattoo in honour of his beloved daughter.
