What is Marysol Patton's net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Slice and The Richest, Marysol Patton has an alleged net worth of between $14 million and $15 million. How did Marysol make her money? She made money through business ventures, reality TV, investments and public relations. Here is a detailed breakdown of the reality TV star's earnings.

Public relations career

According to her LinkedIn profile, Marysol was the CEO and owner of The Patton Group, a PR and events company, from April 2000 to 2016, when she retired. Her position as owner of a renowned and successful company earned her considerably.

During an interview with Refinery29 in 2012, Marysol revealed how she ventured into public relations.

I fell on this accidentally while I was on the board of a charity as a volunteer, and the director of events left, and they asked me to take it. I didn't really have much of an idea on what to do; I just knew that I needed to throw some parties that people would want to buy tickets to.

Inheritance

Marysol grew up in a home surrounded by luxury and wealth. Her father was a well-known yacht builder who had clients across the world. Marysol's mother, Elsa Patton, was part of Miami's socialite class and founded the Havana Elsa Coffee brand.

After the passing of her parents, father in 2018 and mother in 2019, Marysol and her brother inherited a portion of their wealth from their parents. According to Daily Mail, Marysol and her brother sold between $2.3 million and $3.5 million of their mother's belongings in June 2021.

The star revealed in a statement to Page Six that her parents were prominent collectors of antiques, art and jewellery. It reads,

My parents were big collectors of antiques, art and jewelry and had so many pieces across their three homes. My brother and I kept the pieces we really cherish, but so many other pieces were sitting unused. We decided to give the opportunity to others who share my parents' passion for antiques and jewels.
