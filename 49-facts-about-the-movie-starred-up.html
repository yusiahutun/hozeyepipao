FAQs

Q: Is Starred Up based on a true story?

A: No, Starred Up is not based on a true story. However, it is inspired by the real-life experiences of former prison therapist Jonathan Asser, who drew from his own encounters working in British prisons to write the screenplay.

Q: Is the movie suitable for all audiences?

A: Starred Up is rated R for its explicit violence, strong language, and adult themes. It is not recommended for younger viewers or those who are sensitive to graphic content.

Q: Can I watch Starred Up online?

A: Yes, Starred Up is available for streaming on various platforms such as Amazon Prime, Netflix, and Hulu. You can also rent or purchase the movie from online stores like iTunes or Google Play.

Q: What awards did Starred Up receive?

A: Starred Up received critical acclaim and was nominated for several awards. It won the Best Actor Award at the British Independent Film Awards for Jack O’Connell’s outstanding performance. The film also received nominations for Best Supporting Actor and Best Supporting Actress, among others.
