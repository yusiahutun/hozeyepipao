He came out after they divorced in 1999, following 21 years of marriage.

But during an event earlier this week Fran Drescher took the time to set the record straight about some of the more intimate details of her former relationship with Peter Marc Jacobson.

The 57-year-old admitted that people often ask how she didn't know that Peter was gay and she revealed on Tuesday night that the two actually had a very active sex life.

The actress received an honour at the Stonewall Community Foundation Vision Awards and made the confession during her acceptance speech. 

'I have a gay art dealer, a gay dermatologist - not to mention my hairdresser,' she said according to Page Six. 

'I have a gay ex-husband! People always say to me, "How did you not know?" He loves decorating and fashion and clothes, but we actually did have sex a lot.'

Sweethearts: Fran and Jacob met in high school then got married at 21, pictured in New York in 2013

Fran and Jacob met in high school then got married at 21. He is the writer and creator behind her hit series The Nanny that shot her to fame.

The Nineties sitcom was loosely based on Fran's life and saw her character go from working in a bridal store to working for a wealthy widower as a nanny, who she eventually falls in love with. 

The former couple remain best friends and business partners and Fran even attempted to help find him a new mate on The Millionaire Matchmaker.

Hooking him up: The actress appeared on Millionaire Matchmaker in an effort to help her ex Peter find a new mate

Meanwhile, the television star married Shiva Ayyadurai last year in a private ceremony in Malibu, California.  

The pair met in 2013 when Dr. Ayyadurai - who owns the patent to email and is often credited as the inventor of the electronic mail system amid some controversy - was giving a talk at an event hosted by Deepak Chopra.

The inventor told Huffington Post: 'Fran heard my talk and we fell in love, and we've been together since that talk.

'Every day is a celebration with Fran.

'Every day is almost a romantic hangout with her. We're always laughing, always enjoying ourselves.'
