When performing NTSA vehicle registration status check, it is advisable to make service payments of KEs. 550 through Airtel Money, VISA cards, and M-Pesa. Firstly register or login to eCitizen account if you already have an activated account. Once logged in, select National Transport Safety Authority then click on “Get Service Now”. Further, select “Make Application” followed by “Motor Vehicle Registration”.

From the menu, choose “Motor Vehicle Search (Copy of Records)” then press apply. You will eventually be asked to enter the vehicle registration number in the format “KAA787A”. Continue by selecting the mode of payment then follow the prompts to complete the process and download all the necessary vehicle details.

There are several reasons why Kenya Revenue Authority and NTSA motor vehicle authority insist on checking the registration status of a car before changing the ownership. Without a proper vehicle search, a buyer can easily become scammed and conned a lump sum. Online search is helpful in finding out what is mainly contained in the database of the registrar of motor vehicle and about the car. The process equally confirm the contents of the NTSA logbook search as a true replica of the official database. Vehicle details accessed during the vehicle search process has greatly minimized cases of vehicle theft and improper identity.
