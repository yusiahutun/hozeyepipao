    JLo’s affordable collection of affordable sunglasses is pretty impressive and one of her fave low-cost styles just happens to be on sale for 30% off right now: Le Specs’ ‘The Thirst’ shades in rose quartz - sale price $49.99, or £38.46 in the UK.

    I mean, come on – they're sitting in JLo's collection along with her $800 luxury go-tos by Emmanuelle Khanh and Valentino.
