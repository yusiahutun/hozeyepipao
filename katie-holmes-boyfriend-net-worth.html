Parents of Katie Holmes

Katie Holmes was born as Katherine Noelle Holmes on December 18, 1978, in Toledo, Ohio, USA. As of 2019, she is 41 years old and her sun sign is Sagittarius. She is the youngest of five children of Martin Joseph Holmes and Kathleen Holmes.

Siblings of Katie Holmes

She has two elder sisters namely Tamera, Holly, and Nancy and an elder brother, Martin Joseph Holmes, Jr. Her father is an American attorney and her mother is a homemaker and philanthropist. Katie holds an American nationality and she is of mixed ethnicity(German, Irish, English).

Career

  • Holmes started her modeling career as a teenage model with the International Modeling and Talent Association (IMTA) Competition held in New York City in 1996.
  • She made her first professional screen debut in 1997, playing Libbets Casey in The Ice Storm.
  • Her performance was highly appreciated by the audience and filmmakers and she earned many other roles in movies.
  • After one year, she appeared as Rachel Wagner in Disturbing Behavior (1998) for which she was nominated for MTV Movie Award for Best Breakthrough Performance and Saturn Award for Best Performance by a Younger Actor.
  • She got her first big break and her first lead role in 1999 in the movie Teaching Mrs. Tingle.
  • Her role as Leigh Ann Watson in that movie earned her nominations for MTV Movie Award for Best Kiss and Teen Choice Award-Choice Chemistry.
  • In addition, she made her Television debut with the lead role of Joey Potter in Dawson’s Creek (1998-2003).
  • Her first TV performance earned her several awards nominations including Teen Choice Award for Choice TV Actress.
  • She played Rachel Dawes in Batman Begins (2005), for which she earned nominations for Saturn Award for Best Supporting Actress and Golden Raspberry Award for Worst Supporting Actress that year.
  • Her first work as a producer came up with the 2010 movie The Romantics.
  • Recently, she appeared as Rita Carmichael in All We Had (2016), in which she worked as the director and producer as well.
