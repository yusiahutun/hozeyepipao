Margot Robbie Embodies Barbie in a Pink Polka-Dot Minidress

June 29, 2024
blog

Margot Robbie and Ryan Gosling kicked off their Barbie press tour over the weekend—and their looks have been just as pink, polished, and perfect as we all imagined.

On Sunday, the two attended a photo call for the movie at the Four Seasons Hotel in Beverly Hills. For the occasion, Robbie wore a bright pink halter minidress in a white polka-dot print with a cutout in the center. She styled it with white pointed-toe pumps, a large faux pearl bracelet, and a quilted leather yellow mini shoulder bag. She wore a short soft-pink manicure, rosy makeup, and a silky, wavy hairstyle.

Jon Kopaloff//Getty Images

Gosling, meanwhile, looked every bit a cool, modern-day Ken in a breezy beige cardigan with a swirly, orangey pattern, layered over a classic white tee. For bottoms, he wore neat black jeans, a distressed brown leather belt, and black leather boots. His blond hair was styled in a wavy Ken-esque hairdo.

Jon Kopaloff//Getty Images

The costars attended the first press event for Greta Gerwig’s highly anticipated film in L.A. one day earlier, with Robbie pulling up in a life-size version of one of the doll’s most cherished accessories: her pink convertible. The film’s official Instagram account shared two photos of the star posing with a vintage, bubblegum-pink Corvette while dressed in a sunny Barbie-pink co-ord.

The Barbie movie project will mark the first live-action film from the Barbie franchise, and the first movie deal from Mattel Films, the film division of the company behind Barbie and other iconic toys. It is set for release on July 21.
