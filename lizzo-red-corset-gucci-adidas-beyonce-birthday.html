Lizzo Sizzles in a Red-Hot Gucci x Adidas Corset at Beyonc's Birthday Party

March 11, 2024
blog

What's the best way to celebrate our reigning Virgo queen? By partying in style, of course.

Lizzo did just that while attending Beyoncé's star-studded 41st birthday party this past weekend. The "About Damn Time" singer showed up at the lavish fête in a look straight from Gucci's recent viral collaboration with Adidas.

She opted for a red-hot corset, which bore the sportswear label's iconic three white lines as well as Gucci's brown monogram. She paired the statement piece with a matching red miniskirt with a leg slit, and further accessorized with a black headband, brown pumps, a brown box handbag, oversized tortoiseshell sunnies, and plenty of gold jewelry.

Lizzo shared the look on Instagram, aptly captioning the photos, "We Gucci✨"

The Italian fashion house's collaboration with Adidas was first teased on the runway during their Milan fashion show this past February. For the launch of the collection, Gucci's creative director Alessandro Michele found inspiration in the archival 1979 adidas catalogue. “When your thoughts become reality, you understand what magic is,” he said, per a press release.

A statement on Gucci's website elaborates, "Adidas x Gucci merges the emblems of the House with those of the iconic sportswear brand. The collection expands on the sartorial streetwear creations with a spectrum of sport-inspired pieces in which the heritage of both brands is encoded in a trio of lines."

Michele had also reworked Adidas's signature Gazelle shoes. The site explains, "A silhouette that has become iconic thanks to its essential and versatile form, all the while looking to the future, the emblematic adidas Gazelle is reworked through Alessandro Michele's creative vision. For the occasion many versions have been presented, including velvet, snake print, and GG monogram variations."
