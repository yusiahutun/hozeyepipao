The History of Helmond Sport

Helmond Sport is a professional football club based in Helmond, Netherlands. The club was founded in 1967 and has since become a vital part of the Dutch football scene. Known for its rich history and passionate fanbase, Helmond Sport boasts a legacy that spans over five decades.
