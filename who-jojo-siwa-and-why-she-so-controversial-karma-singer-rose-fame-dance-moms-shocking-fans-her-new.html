Here’s a look inside her backstory and biggest controversies:

Why is JoJo Siwa famous?

Siwa rose to fame after appearing on Dance Moms in 2015 as a 12-year-old, per People. She then grew her fan base by starting a YouTube channel, and launched a music career not long after.Noticing Siwa’s popularity on YouTube and Instagram, Nickelodeon signed a lucrative deal with the then-14-year-old influencer in 2017, which included consumer products, original programming, social media, live events and music, according to Business Wire. Since joining the network, Siwa has guest-starred in TV series’ and films. She has also been featured in several Nickelodeon specials.

What’s JoJo Siwa doing now?

Siwa seems to be focused on her new music at the moment. “It’s giving you Karma. This costume is my baby. This is what I wear in the official ‘Karma’ music video,” she said on the red carpet at the recent iHeartRadio Music Awards. “This is a big ole sneak peak right here,” she added as she showed off a sparkly sheer bodysuit and face make-up reminiscent of 70s rock band Kiss.

How many girlfriends has JoJo Siwa had?

Since coming out in January 2021, Siwa has been romantically linked to influencers Kylie Prew, Katie Mills and Avery Cyrus, according to British media.

What are JoJo Siwa’s biggest controversies?

Siwa has come under scrutiny for a handful of controversies in recent years. According to OK!, her make-up collaboration with Claire’s had to be recalled by the US Food and Drug Administration due to asbestos concerns, and she also came under fire for a circus-themed music video which featured a dancer accused of appearing in blackface, which she disputed. She’s also had reported feuds with Justin Bieber and rapper DaBaby.
