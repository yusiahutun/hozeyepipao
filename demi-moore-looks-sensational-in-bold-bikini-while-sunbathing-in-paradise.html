Demi Moore looks sensational in bold swimsuit while sunbathing in 'paradise'

August 23, 2024
blog

Film star Demi Moore has shown she still has it with a recent stunning photo of her swimsuit while enjoying the rays while in “paradise”.

The celebrity, who was formerly married to Bruce Willis, has shared with fans inside her recent trip as she enjoys “basking in the sun”. Her bold swimsuit is teased in the photo as she smiles from ear to ear with her pup laying on her chest.

Demi Moore greets fans with swimsuit photo

The Ghost actor took to Instagram to share the gorgeous photos with her followers on Tuesday, August 9. Demi also gave insight into what she enjoys doing while on vacation with those closest and dearest.

She updated fans that she sunbathes, takes a dip in the water, reads “good” books and snoozes off when she gets a chance.

The movie star captioned the photo with: “Paradise. Basking in the sun, playing in the water, naps with furry friends, and a good book”. Beside Demi is her chihuahua Pilaf, but it’s believed she may have as many as 10 rescue dogs. She also has a Yorkie and a Dachshund, and often shares snaps of the pack.

Behind the Hollywood icon was a sensational view of the coast. Rich blue skies were on the horizon with clear water as well as faint indications of mountains. It’s proving to be the best backdrop for an Instagram photo dump.

While Demi’s full fit wasn’t in view, we can see her colorful bikini complimented her sun kissed complexion. She wore oversized shades to protect her from the sunshine and wore her hair back in a messy bun. No clouds were in sight – perfection.

In the second photo, she revealed her pedicure matched her swimsuit with bright red nail polish. It also unveiled she was relaxing next to a stunning infinity pool.

Paradise after supporting writer’s strike

Demi, 60, divulged more picturesque snaps on her Instagram Stories, with a view of the coast and rippling water, delicious ripe tomatoes and told fans she’s currently got her nose in Mirinae Lee’s book, 8 Lives of a Century-Old Trickster.

Although the mom-of-three didn’t reveal where she’s jet setted off to, she recently showed support during the Writer’s Strike taking place in Hollywood.

The GI Jane actor was “proud” to walk with fellow SAG-AFTRA members, who are currently striking over a dispute on pay and job security. The labor strike currently underway with the American actors’ union is against the Alliance of Motion Picture and Television Producers. It has coincided with the ongoing strike with the Writers Guild of America, pushing Hollywood into chaos. It’s seen TV shows on hiatus, like Netflix’s hit Stranger Things, as well as funny man Jimmy Fallon’s nighttime talk show.

It has dragged on since July 14 and doesn’t see any sign of stopping just yet. However, actors, comedians, movie stars and celebrity legends have come out to support all those striking.
