Kendall Jenner Wore a Black Latex Turtleneck Under Her Emmys Gown

September 29, 2024
blog

Hot off the heels of Milan Fashion Week, Kendall Jenner made a surprise appearance at tonight's Emmy Awards. The model, who already ditched her newly-blonde hair, arrived to the red carpet wearing a red floral Richard Quinn gown with a black latex turtleneck bodysuit underneath.

Alberto E. Rodriguez//Getty Images

Kendall Jenner in Richard Quinn at the Emmys.

The risk-taking red carpet look is straight off Richard Quinn's Spring 2020 runway, which took place at London Fashion Week just last week. Just like its runway styling, Kendall opted to keep the black latex turtleneck underneath the rose print mermaid gown.

Victor VIRGILE//Getty Images

The model attended the award show with her sister Kim Kardashian West, who opted for a classic black gown accessorized with silver chains and a cross necklace. After walking the red carpet together, the duo presented the Emmy Award for 'Outstanding Competition Program’ during the ceremony.

Fox//Getty Images

Kendall Jenner and Kim Kardashian West attend the Emmys together.

From pink and red colorblocked gowns to Gwendoline Christie's Game of Thrones-inspired Gucci cloak, there were plenty of bold red carpet looks at this year's Emmys. But of all the gowns and micro trends that emerged, it's safe to say Kendall was the only one wearing black latex. And unsurprisingly, the 23-year-old model completely pulled it off.
