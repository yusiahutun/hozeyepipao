Dolly Parton Is Launching Her Own Fashion Line So You Can Dress Like the Icon: I Am Excited

March 05, 2024
blog

You all ready to dress like Dolly Parton? The legendary performer is set to launch her own fashion line!

According to Women’s Wear Daily, the 73-year-old has signed her first exclusive global licensing deal with IMG, and will begin building quite the lifestyle empire. As part of this launch, the singer will sell clothing, jewelry, accessories and even home goods.

“I am excited to be working with IMG on a global scale to give my fans products that they will cherish for years to come,” the 9 to 5 actress said in a statement to the outlet. “You might even see my mug on a mug.”

“Dolly Parton is an international icon and we are thrilled to be working with her,” vp licensing Gary Krakower said in a statement. “Together, we look forward to building cohesive lifestyle brand products that will celebrate Dolly and bring her iconic style and personality to her millions of fans worldwide in engaging new ways.”

Dolly is already quite known for donning everything from bright colors to wigs to sequins on stage, so one can only imagine the outfits her line will include. And while she’s been in the spotlight for years, she would still give her younger self a bit of fashion advice.

“Wear more wigs! And makeup! More rhinestones!” she exclusively told Closer Weekly with a laugh. “And keep going. Write more songs. Do more movies!” The Dollywood owner has plenty of hits, films and accolades too, but she also said that she still gets starstruck at Hollywood events.

“The parties and seeing all the stars — I still get a kick out of it,” she told Closer Weekly at the
Variety Television Nominees Celebration. “I’m a country girl. I used to think about Hollywood when I’d see it on TV. Now I get to be part of it. It’s fun.”

We can’t wait to see what else is up Dolly’s sleeve!
