Adam Sandler Launches Fresher Tour

June 26, 2024
blog

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) Adam Sandler has announced he will travel North America this summer on the 100% Fresher Tour, hitting 18 cities starting with Chicago June 1.

Sandler has been performing to sold-out shows over the past couple of years. 100% Fresh was released on Netflix on Oct. 23 and marked Sandler’s first comedy special in 20 years. His newest film, “Murder Mystery,” will stream on Netflix June 14.

Tickets go on sale at LiveNation.com on Friday, March 29.
